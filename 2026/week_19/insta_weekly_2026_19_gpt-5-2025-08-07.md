# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway (May 4–10, 2026): Glitter-forward, alt-beauty meets ethereal fairycore. Multichrome toppers, flakies, and grayscale lips drove the week, while tutorial-style reels (cloud liner, nose contour, lip combos) outperformed static looks and fueled purchase intent.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - Tutorial-heavy reels with clear step-by-steps (cloud eyeliner, eyeliner stamps, nose contour, lip layering) plus swatch demos of chromes/baked shimmers.
  - Alt-beauty aesthetics (goth/grunge, cyber-goth) blended with whimsical fairy elements (butterfly wings, “pixie dust” pigments, periwinkle lashes).
  - Fragrance detour performed strongly (layering sets and affordable standouts).

- Engagement Temperature:
  - High excitement and curiosity. Repeated “restock?” and “make it permanent” comments; shade-specific Qs and comparisons (“Is the texture like PMG baked?”).
  - Active tagging/friend-sharing on sparkly textures and unique tech (non-burning, “vibe” lip plumper).
  - Occasional pushback on overconsumption, but sentiment net positive.

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Sparkle stack:
    - Multichrome toppers: Clionadh (Regal, Flash(ed) Glass, Orchard), Terra Moons (Vampire Star), Karla Cosmetics (Dreamer).
    - Flakie gels/liners: Dieverune (Shiver) + Half Magic Glitter Puck (Feel Something), Magic Flik.
  - Alt lips (grayscale/blackened):
    - Bella Beauté Bar x Freya (Flux, Undivided), Dieverune Bitter, Experiment Void; include a true-grey liner and a black liner.
  - White creams/liquids for graphic details:
    - Danessa Myricks Colorfix Paint (Lift) or comparable budge-proof whites for cloud looks.
  - Mascaras beyond black:
    - Teased periwinkle/grey (Glisten), blue (MAC Denim Stack), UD Tube Job.
  - If fragrance is in-scope: The 7 Virtues layering pairs (Vanilla Woods + Strawberry Jam) and discovery sets.

- Content Strategy:
  - Double down on 45–90s reels that teach a single technique (e.g., cloud liner over brown base, stamp eyeliner placement, nose “bron-tour” using leftovers).
  - Swatch sunlight macros of chromes/baked shimmers (show shifts on multiple skin tones); pair with “shade chooser” captions.
  - Theme prompts (butterfly, blue goldstone, oil slick) to drive UGC; repost best looks.
  - “Alt lip wardrobe” try-ons (sheer black → slate mud → sparkle black gloss) with side-by-side comparisons and liner recipes.

- Gap Analysis:
  - Long-wear eye prep: bundle waxy eye bases/sticks and tubing mascaras to solve smudging (echoing Robert Welsh’s education).
  - Drugstore bright editorial shades missing (e.g., true red blush, discontinued alt lipsticks) — curate indie alternatives and call it out explicitly.
  - Non-burning plumpers: prioritize Tower 28 “vibe plump” upon launch; offer waitlist and education (what to expect/feel).

## 6. Special insights

- Emerging Micro-Trends:
  - Baked shimmer revival via C-beauty (Spenny) — dry-texture, high-shift domes resurfacing with nostalgia narrative.
  - Colored/neutralized mascaras (periwinkle, denim blue, pending grey) as an easy entry to editorial looks.
  - Grayscale lip category normalizing for daily alt wear (sheer black balms to slate muds).

- Unexpected Pairings:
  - Green eyeshadow used as lip liner to harmonize fantasy skin tones (Frankiestein cosplay); blue balms to cool nudes; pink setting powder lines to sculpt nose highlights.
  - Chartreuse transition + periwinkle lid shimmer — nature-inspired yet cyber in finish.

- Creative Insight:
  - Stone/object-inspired palettes (blue goldstone, hydrangea florals) resonate: package “inspo-to-kit” bundles linking an image/mood to exact shades (matte base + one high-shift topper + liner + accent mascara). It bridges editorial fantasy with shoppable minimal SKUs.
