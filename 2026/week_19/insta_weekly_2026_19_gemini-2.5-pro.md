# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

*   **Key Takeaway:** For the week ending May 10, 2026, the indie beauty landscape was dominated by cool-toned, high-texture aesthetics, blending grunge (#grungemakeup) with ethereal whimsy (#etherealmakeup). The launch of Tower 28's novel "vibrating" lip plumper generated significant conversational buzz, while collaborations like Bella Beauté Bar x Freya drove momentum in the alt-beauty space.

## 1. Overview

*   **The Vibe:** The feed was highly dynamic, prioritizing video content. Creators favored tutorial-style Reels demonstrating specific techniques (e.g., "cloud makeup," "nose contour") and swatch-focused videos that served as deep dives into specific color stories (e.g., Clionadh's purples, Spenny's baked shimmers). Static images were reserved for showcasing finished, editorial-style looks with complex artistry.

*   **Engagement Temperature:** Overwhelmingly positive and community-focused. While general compliments ("stunning," "gorgeous") were abundant, pointed engagement clustered around novel or unique products. The "buzzy" sensation of the new Tower 28 lip plumper sparked significant curiosity, and followers showed strong interest in specific items like Glisten Cosmetics' upcoming grey mascara.

## 2. Product Momentum (Brand & Item Velocity)

*   **Top Mentioned Brands:** Bella Beauté Bar, Glisten Cosmetics, Half Magic Beauty, Danessa Myricks Beauty, Rituel de Fille, Unearthly Cosmetics, and Tower 28 Beauty consistently appeared across multiple influential creators.

*   **Viral Products:**
    *   **Tower 28 "Vibe Plump" Lip Plumper:** This product launch was the week's main event. Its unique "vibrating" or "TV static" sensation, marketed as a pain-free plumping alternative, drove high-view videos and specific questions from followers about the experience.
    *   **Bella Beauté Bar x @freya86_ff "Yin & Yang" Collection:** This collaboration gained significant traction, appearing in multiple looks. Its cool-toned, grungy palette and unique grey/black lip products ("Flux" gloss, "Undivided" lip mud) are becoming staples for alt-beauty creators.
    *   **Half Magic Beauty x Euphoria Collection:** The glitter products, particularly the "Glitterpuck" and "Glitter Tears" liner, were heavily featured in high-impact, textured eye looks, cementing the brand's association with bold, artistic expression.

*   **The "Look" Breakdown:**
    *   **EYES:** Texture is the hero. Multichromes, duochromes, and chunky glitter/flakie gels are the dominant finishes. Cool-toned palettes featuring purples, blues, periwinkles, and mossy greens were most prevalent.
    *   **LIPS:** A strong dichotomy is emerging. On one end, moody, grungy lips featuring grey, black, and deep red shades, often defined with a black liner. On the other, ultra-high-shine, wet-look glosses.
    *   **CHEEKS:** Highlighters are non-negotiable, often with an iridescent or duochrome finish (e.g., Dieverune's violet-blue "Drop" highlight). Blush is applied softly or draped high on the cheekbones in bold pink tones.

## 3. Aesthetic & Hashtag Trends

*   **Visual Themes:** The intersection of dark and whimsical aesthetics is clear. Hashtags like **#gothmakeup**, **#grungemakeup**, and **#alternativemakeup** were frequently paired with **#etherealmakeup** and **#sparklymakeup**. This blend manifests as "celestial grunge" or "fairy goth" looks. The "Frankie Stein" cosplay by @ghoul.in.japan perfectly captured this hybrid vibe.

*   **Color Stories:**
    *   **Celestial Grunge:** Deep indigos, violets, and sparkling silvers were used to create looks inspired by "blue goldstone" and the night sky.
    *   **Mossy Greens & Teals:** A community-driven challenge (#supernovainstallations) pushed a color story of mossy greens, slate greys, and silvers, resulting in a wave of coordinated grungy looks.
    *   **Monochromatic Cool Tones:** Looks built entirely around shades of purple or blue were common, with creators using matching eyeshadow, highlighter, and even mascara.

*   **Curiosity Clusters:**
    *   **Unique Formulas:** Followers asked technical questions about the Tower 28 plumper ("How long does it vibrate for??") and Spenny baked shimmers ("Is the texture and feel like PMG baked shades 👀👀").
    *   **New Colorways:** The potential for a new grey mascara from Glisten Cosmetics generated significant excitement: "Can’t wait for Glisten to release the grey mascara!!!!"

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

*   **Stocking Recommendations:**
    *   **Prioritize Texture:** Focus on high-impact, textural eyeshadows. Multichromes, duochromes, and baked/jelly shimmers from brands like Clionadh Cosmetics, Terra Moons, and emerging C-Beauty brands are in high demand.
    *   **Curate Alt-Lip Colors:** There is a clear, consistent demand for non-traditional lip colors. Stocking a range of cool-toned greys, blacks, and taupes in various finishes (gloss, matte "mud," balm) would meet this need.
    *   **Expand Colored Mascara/Liner:** Move beyond black. Unique shades like periwinkle, grey, and deep blue for mascaras and liners are being used as key details in trending looks.

*   **Content Strategy:** High-performing content is educational and visually dynamic. Prioritize creating tutorial-style Reels that demystify a technique (nose contouring) or showcase the versatility of a unique product (multichrome pigment). Swatch videos focused on a single color family are also highly effective for this audience.

*   **Gap Analysis:** The recurring theme of searching for the "alt beauty lover" lip product indicates a market gap. While creators are finding options, a one-stop-shop for curated, high-quality alternative lip shades is an untapped opportunity.

## 6. Special insights

*   **Emerging Micro-Trends:**
    *   **The "Lazy" Contour:** A tutorial from @linsmakeuplooks demonstrated nose contouring using only the leftover product on existing bronzer and blush brushes. This accessible, low-effort technique lowers the barrier to entry for a traditionally intimidating step.
    *   **Contrasting Finishes:** Creator @s.andromedae explicitly highlighted her preference for pairing a "super glowy eye alongside a super matte lip," signaling a potential shift away from monochromatic texture trends.

*   **Unexpected Pairings:**
    *   Creators are using products beyond their intended purpose. In a "Frankie Stein" look, green eyeshadow was used as a "nude" lip liner to match the green skin base, showcasing advanced color theory and resourcefulness.

*   **Creative insight**:
    *   **Sensory-Driven Beauty:** The Tower 28 plumper's success hinges on its unique physical sensation ("buzzy," "vibrating"), not just its visual effect. This indicates an opportunity for products that offer a novel sensory experience as a key selling point.
    *   **Community-Led Trend Cycles:** The #supernovainstallations challenge demonstrates how a single creator can initiate a micro-trend by issuing a color palette prompt, effectively directing the creative output of a niche community for a set period.
    *   **Nostalgia as a Formula:** The repeated description of "baked shimmers" as "nostalgic" suggests that specific product textures can trigger powerful emotional connections to past beauty eras. Marketing certain formulas by evoking this nostalgia could be highly effective.
