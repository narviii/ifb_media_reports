# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** May 04–10, 2026

1. **Yin & Yang Palette by Bella Beauté Bar** — 7 mentions  
   Creators expressed high excitement for this collaboration, frequently praising its cool-toned, "grungified" aesthetic and metallic, holographic shimmer finishes. It was heavily featured in smoky, celestial, and alternative makeup looks across platforms, with users noting its opaque pigmentation and smooth application.

2. **Flux Lip Gloss by Bella Beauté Bar** — 6 mentions  
   Highlighted as a standout shade, this gloss was praised for its unique grey-purple tones and subtle but noticeable sparkle. Creators frequently used it to seal lip combos, noting that it beautifully enhanced the cool and purple tones of their overall alternative makeup looks.

3. **Moonlit Creature of Light by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   This product was consistently utilized as a luminous primer or base to create a radiant, ethereal complexion. Creators favored it for its ability to provide a glowing, dewy finish that perfectly complemented high-contrast, moody, and celestial-themed editorial looks.

4. **Powder Melt Spray by ONE/SIZE** — 5 mentions  
   A staple in several creators' routines, this setting spray was repeatedly used to lock in intricate, full-glam, and editorial makeup applications. It was trusted to melt powder products seamlessly into the skin, ensuring longevity for heavy, multi-layered looks.

5. **Undivided Lip Mud by Bella Beauté Bar** — 4 mentions  
   Described as a highly pigmented slate grey, this lip mud was favored for its comfortable, "moosey" texture that blends effortlessly with lip liners. Creators loved its muted, grungy aesthetic, often pairing it with deeper liners to create striking, alternative lip combinations.

6. **Intuition by Rituel de Fille** — 4 mentions  
   This product was heavily relied upon as a cool-toned contour to sculpt the face in alternative and monochromatic makeup styles. Creators appreciated its ability to blend softly into the skin, providing a natural-looking shadow that suited desaturated and moody color palettes.

7. **Thorn Milk Mist by Rituel de Fille** — 4 mentions  
   Frequently featured alongside other products from the brand, this mist was used as a hydrating and finishing step in complex editorial routines. It helped creators achieve a soft-focus, skin-like finish while maintaining the integrity of their heavy, stylized makeup.

8. **Spectra Pencil in Black by Glisten Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   This black pencil was a go-to for defining and adding depth to moody, grunge-inspired lip combos. Creators praised its versatility, often using it daily to contour the corners of the lips and create a striking, sultry gradient effect.

9. **Taupe Speckled Pen by Glisten Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   This pen was a favorite for adding realistic faux freckles to the skin, bringing a natural, textured element to heavy editorial makeup. Creators consistently used its soft taupe shade to complement their high-glam, cool-toned looks with a touch of youthful, lived-in detail.

10. **Baked Shimmers by Spenny** — 3 mentions  
    These C-beauty single shadows evoked strong nostalgia among creators, who praised their highly reflective, multidimensional duochrome shifts. The formula was celebrated for its buildable opacity and intense sparkle, particularly in shades featuring electric blue and green shifts.
