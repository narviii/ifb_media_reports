# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** June 01–07, 2026

1. **Arch of Color Collection by BellaBeauteBar** — 13 mentions  
   This highly anticipated, rainbow-themed collection—featuring a 36-pan all-matte palette, 15 multichrome singles, and sparkly lip balms—dominated creator feeds. Reviewers praised its nostalgic, early-2000s "scene girl" aesthetic and the intense, smooth pigmentation of the mattes. The magnetic packaging and high-shine, textured shimmer singles were frequently highlighted as standout features in dedicated swatch videos and tutorials.

2. **Psilocybin Collection by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 11 mentions  
   This mushroom and fairy-themed collection generated strong enthusiasm for its grungy yet vibrant color story. Creators frequently used the palette to craft whimsical, colorful eye looks, praising the blendability of the mattes and the high-impact sparkle of the shimmers. The collection’s accompanying lip glazes and cheek palettes were also positively featured as staples in full-face routines.

3. **Space Candy Collection by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
   A summer-themed collection of 14 single eyeshadows that captivated creators with its intense, multi-dimensional sparkle and duochrome shifts. Reviewers heavily swatched the shades, noting the "wet-look" finish and highlighting specific favorites like "Cotton Candy Cosmos" and "Cherry Comet." The formula was consistently praised for its high reflectivity and smooth application over glitter primers.

4. **The Huldra Palette by SINGE Beauty** — 6 mentions  
   Featured as a prominent new release, this palette was celebrated for its unique mix of earthy neutrals, swampy greens, and jewel tones with a grunge twist. Creators demonstrated its versatility by creating dark, smoky, and sparkly eye looks, noting the beautiful contrast between the matte greens and the purple/blue shimmers. The sentiment was overwhelmingly positive, with users loving the cohesive, moody color story.

5. **Eye Primer by Lethal Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   This eye primer served as a staple base product in several creators' routines, particularly for vibrant, graphic, and heavy shimmer eye looks. It was consistently listed in product breakdowns alongside affiliate codes, indicating strong creator trust and ongoing brand partnerships. The recurring inclusion highlights its reliability for gripping intense indie pigments without creasing.

6. **Blood Splatter Primer by Lethal Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   Used frequently as a go-to face base, this primer was a recurring favorite in daily makeup details. Creators implicitly endorsed it by consistently incorporating it into their full-face routines alongside other trusted indie and mainstream complexion products. Its repeated use across multiple videos and posts suggests it performs exceptionally well for long-lasting makeup applications.

7. **Freckle Pen by Nabla Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   This faux freckle product was a popular finishing touch for creators aiming for a natural, sun-kissed, or ethereal aesthetic. It was frequently mentioned in product breakdowns and used to add scattered freckles across the nose and cheeks, complementing both soft glam and heavy editorial looks. The sentiment was highly positive, cementing it as a reliable tool for the ongoing faux-freckle trend.

8. **Moonlit Creature of Light by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   Used primarily as a luminous primer or full-face serum, this product was favored for creating a dewy, glowing base. Creators incorporated it into highly editorial and creative makeup looks to achieve a "glass skin" effect that paired beautifully with intense, graphic eye makeup. Its consistent use underscores its effectiveness in providing a radiant, hydrated complexion.

9. **Strawberry Picnic Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This upcoming release charmed creators with its cute, summer-ready theme and a color story split between warm berry tones and earthy greens. Reviewers praised the improved, opaque matte formula and the smooth, highly reflective duochrome shimmers. The palette generated strong excitement for its cohesive aesthetic and high-quality performance in dedicated swatch and review content.

10. **Electric Carnival Palette by Nomad Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    Launched during the reporting period, this all-chrome palette was highlighted for its beautiful, shifty shimmers. Creators showcased the palette in dedicated swatch videos, expressing amazement at the multi-dimensional, high-impact finish of shades like "Unity" and "Pixie Haze." The response was highly positive, with users eager to incorporate the vibrant multichromes into their colorful routines.
