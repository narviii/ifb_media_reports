# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway: June 1–7, 2026 pulsed with pre‑launch sparkle hype and Y2K nostalgia. Multichrome toppers and bold blues led the feed as Terra Moons Space Candy and Bella Beauté Bar’s Arch of Color drove comment‑level buying questions; tutorial reels outperformed static looks.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - Heavy on swatches + side‑by‑side shade breakdowns (Space Candy, Arch of Color, Strawberry Picnic).
  - Tutorial Reels for colorful, editorial eyes (graphic liners, two‑tone Pride looks, neon inner corners) and quick skin technique clips (concealer brightening, sponge/blending, lash safety).
  - Nostalgic relaunch moments (Marc Jacobs Beauty) and trade‑show lifestyle (Premiere Orlando; Glisten Cosmetics at Paris).
- Engagement Temperature:
  - High excitement with specific purchase questions (“differences between Limelight vs Lunar Lemon?” “does Cherry stain?” “do they crease?”).
  - “Add to cart” energy across sparkle launches and alt lip shades; multiple “send me”/“need this” comments and restock chatter (Clionadh Glow Quad, Pastel Garden).

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Sparkle toppers and singles: Terra Moons Space Candy, with hero shades (Raspberry Cluster, Wildberry Wormhole, Limelight, Galactic Gummies, Cherry Comet, Cotton Candy Cosmos). Consider deeper buy on those mentioned in comments.
  - Rainbow mattes + companion shimmers: Bella Beauté Bar Arch of Color palette + 15 singles; add lip sparkle balms Sunshine (orange) and Daydream (teal) to an “alt lips” endcap.
  - Grunge brights: Blend Bunny Psilocybin palette + cheek palette + hybrid/lip glazes; Singe Beauty Huldra for swampy greens/purples.
  - Blue statement category: MAC Denim Stack (blue), Marc Jacobs Take Notes liner; alt blue/teal lips (Dieverune Blue Iris, Kaleidos Agave, Blend Bunny Blue Tears).
  - Value sparkle: ColourPop Jelly Much Sticks (curate best “wet‑look” shades) + Super Shock bestsellers.
  - Complexion/cheek add‑ons: NYX Buttermilk (Butter Than Cherry Pop), Violette_FR Plume blush, Lisa Eldridge highlighting balm; freckle pens (ColourPop/Nabla).
  - Restock‑sensitive: Clionadh Glow Quad (or comparable topper singles) and emphasize “singles” option to match audience preference.

- Content Strategy:
  - Short, macro swatch reels with shade comparisons answer top DMs (e.g., Limelight vs Lunar Lemon; blue chrome dupes).
  - “On brown skin” wear tests for chrome/toppers and orange/blue lip balms—clear demand and under‑served references.
  - 30–60s mini‑tutorials: double‑wing, chartreuse lower mascara pop, periwinkle+plum combo, “neon inner corner three ways.”
  - Performance testing: crease/wear 8–10 hours for jelly/chrome sticks; “does it stain?” rapid tests for cherry reds.
  - Nostalgia hooks: Y2K/“webcore” liner challenge + Marc Jacobs star/heart packaging flatlays.

- Gap Analysis:
  - Customers want: 
    - Singles availability (Clionadh‑style toppers; Bella’s metallics as singles—stock similar).
    - More orange lip options and safe blue lip formulas (expand alt lip assortment).
    - Deep red matte (Strawberry Picnic lacked)—supplement with singles.
    - Clear EU shipping info/partners; coordinate with Monolith EU where possible.
  - Packaging pain: SheenEffect’s new non‑magnetic packaging negative sentiment—position alternatives with depot‑friendly formats.
  - Education: Glitter/eye safety and lash curl safety resonate—pair with eye‑safe sparkle primers/bases (DD Signature/Unearthly Glimmer Grasp) and tubing mascaras.
