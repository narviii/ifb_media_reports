# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** January 19–25, 2026

1. **Succulent Dew Palette by Singe Beauty** — 16 mentions  
   The launch of this palette dominated conversations, with creators expressing excitement for its jewel-toned color story of teals, pinks, and purples. The formula received overwhelmingly positive feedback, with creators praising the mattes as "super easy to blend" and "intensely pigmented." The shimmers were consistently described as "beautiful," "sparkly," and having a traditional texture that performs well.

2. **Evocative Silk Blush by Singe Beauty** — 13 mentions  
   This blush line extension was highly anticipated, particularly for its new cool-toned and purple shades like "Fearless Fig." Creators lauded the formula for providing a "beautiful glow" and a skin-like, airbrushed finish without being glittery or shimmery. The sheer but buildable pigmentation was a frequently mentioned positive, allowing for controlled application on various skin tones.

3. **Nascent Palette by Starborn Cosmetics** — 12 mentions  
   As the debut launch from creator @badtothebrow, this palette generated significant buzz and positive first impressions. Creators described the color story as "grungy" and "earthy," praising the buildable mattes and "sparkly, twinkly" shimmers. The shimmer formula was noted for being a drier texture that is still multi-dimensional, which some creators preferred for minimizing creasing.

4. **Bleeding Hearts Palette by Gourmande Girls** — 10 mentions  
   This palette was praised for its unique take on a Valentine's theme, mixing traditional pinks and berries with smoky, deep tones like greens and grays. Creators consistently highlighted the high performance of the formula, describing the mattes as "PIGMENTED" and the shimmers as "impactful" and "gorgeous." The palette was frequently used to create intense, well-blended smoky eye looks.

5. **Heart To Get Collection by ColourPop** — 10 mentions  
   This Valentine's collection was featured in numerous creator reviews and tutorials, with a focus on its cool-toned pink and gray aesthetic. The Super Shock Blushes were a standout item, with one creator calling them "damn banging" for their unnatural yet beautiful glow that melts into the skin. While some found the eyeshadow palette left "a little something to be desired," the overall collection was a popular topic for its theme and affordability.

6. **Veil of Winter Skies Singles Collection by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
   This launch of cool-toned singles was well-received by creators who love blue and muted shades. The collection was noted for its mix of pigmented mattes and intensely sparkly shimmers, including new holographic formulas that were praised for their smooth texture and strong effect. Topper shades like "Ice Flare" were highlighted for their versatile, diamond-like sparkle.

7. **Pinky Promise Palette by Bella Beaute Bar** — 7 mentions  
   This collaboration with creator @deandranicoleee was celebrated as the "ultimate pink palette" and a "monochromatic pink explosion." Creators praised its vibrant color story and excellent performance, noting the smooth application and high pigmentation of both mattes and shimmers. A key point of positive sentiment was that the palette was specifically designed to work well on tan, deep, and dark skin tones.

8. **Lip Products (Juicy Tints/Chroma Gloss) by Glisten Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   Glisten's lip products were featured in several posts showcasing unique colors and finishes. The Juicy Tints were used to create vibrant, saturated lip looks in shades like strawberry, grape, and guava. The Chroma Gloss in "Aurora" received a detailed positive review, with the creator impressed by its blue, green, and pink shifts and its comfortable, non-sticky balm-like texture.

9. **Twilight Eclipse Collection by ColourPop** — 3 mentions  
   As a follow-up to a previous successful collaboration, this collection generated positive anticipation among creators. They were intrigued by the moody, cool-toned, and red color story, expressing excitement for ColourPop's well-regarded red matte formula. The quality was expected to be high based on positive experiences with the brand's prior *Twilight* palette.

10. **Eye Pencils by Kaleidos** — 3 mentions  
    These pencils were used by creators to complement and complete various eye looks, often in the waterline. The liners were praised for their performance, with creators noting the "pinky-purple pewter tone" of Aurora Runner complemented a look well, while the metallic red of Scarlet Striker helped enhance red tones in an eye look.
