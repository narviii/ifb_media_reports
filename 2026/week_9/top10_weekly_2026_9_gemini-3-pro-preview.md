# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** February 23–March 01, 2026

1. **Spring Butterfly Collection by Oden’s Eye x Heather Austin** — 28 mentions
   This collaboration dominated the week’s conversation as a highly anticipated launch, with creators praising the "glow-up" in formula featuring buttery mattes and blinding multichromes. Influencers highlighted the collection's versatility, specifically the magnetic design that allows for custom arrangements and the intricate butterfly-themed artwork.

2. **Silver and Cold Collection by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 9 mentions
   Teased ahead of its March 6th launch, this collection was celebrated by creators as a "cool-toned lover's dream." Swatch videos highlighted the "oil slick" intensity of the shimmers—particularly the single shade "Bismuth"—and the unique inclusion of lip muds and mascaras in the lineup.

3. **Glitter Puck by Half Magic** — 5 mentions
   Creators consistently used this product to achieve high-impact, wet-look sparkle on both the eyes and face. Shades like "Princess Ghost" and "Cyber Diamond" were favorites for adding an "ethereal" or "alien" highlight to creative makeup looks.

4. **Spectra Base & Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 5 mentions
   These fluid liners were utilized for their versatility, functioning as both graphic eyeliners and metallic lip products. One creator demonstrated using the gold and silver bases to create fully opaque, transfer-proof "liquid metal" lips, while others used the stamp pens for detailed accents.

5. **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions
   Appearing as a staple in multiple look breakdowns, this mascara was consistently used to define lashes against heavy, glittery eyeshadow looks. It served as a reliable core product for creators focusing on alternative and soft-goth aesthetics.

6. **Dewdrops Palette (Vol 1 & 2) by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 4 mentions
   Creators praised this series for its "blinding" and "ethereal" highlighters, specifically citing shades like "Dew" and "Drop" for their intense ultraviolet and blue-green shifts. The product was noted for its smooth texture and ability to provide an "otherworldly glow."

7. **Dead Roses Palette by Bella Beauté Bar** — 4 mentions
   Ranked highly by creators for its unique "murky decaying plant" theme, this palette was favored for its swampy greens and dusty plum tones. It was described as a "treasure trove" for grungy garden aesthetics, with the accompanying highlighter also receiving praise for its sparkle.

8. **Woeful Whimsy Collection by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 4 mentions
   This upcoming collaboration with creator @s.andromedae generated buzz through "carnival-themed" teasers and shade reveals. The shade "Bitter," a blue-brown shimmer with red sparkles, was specifically highlighted as a perfect "one-and-done" lid shade.

9. **Succulent Dew Palette by Singe Beauty** — 3 mentions
   Multiple creators cited this palette as a favorite for its unique teal and dusty pink color story. It was used to create structural, colorful matte bases for intricate eye looks, with the formula receiving praise for its blendability and pigmentation.

10. **Diabla Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 3 mentions
    Identified by creators as a "most used" monthly favorite, this palette was essential for creating vibrant, blown-out matte looks. Its deep pinks and purples were frequently used to add depth and dimension to colorful, soft-glam styles.
