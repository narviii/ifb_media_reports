# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** February 23–March 01, 2026

1. **Spring Butterfly Collection by Oden’s Eye x Heather Austin** — 20 mentions
   This collaboration dominated the week, with creators praising the formula’s "glow-up," specifically noting the buttery mattes and blinding multichromes. The butterfly-themed packaging and the flexible purchasing model—allowing users to buy the full bundle or build custom palettes with singles—were highlighted as major selling points.

2. **Silver and Cold Collection by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 8 mentions
   Creators expressed high anticipation for this cool-toned launch, describing it as an "alt beauty lover's dream" featuring "oil slick" shimmers and icy highlighters. Influencers like Sini K and Smita focused on the high-impact sparkle of the singles and the cohesive, moody aesthetic of the palette.

3. **Glitter Puck by HALF MAGIC** — 5 mentions
   Showcased primarily on Instagram, this product was celebrated for its versatility as a high-shine topper for eyes, cheeks, and lips. Creators highlighted the "wet-look" finish and intense reflectivity of shades like *Princess Ghost*, noting it adheres well without requiring additional adhesive.

4. **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 4 mentions
   This mascara appeared repeatedly as a staple in creative and alternative makeup looks, particularly by creator Andromeda. It is utilized to ground ethereal, glitter-heavy, and graphic eye looks with a lengthening and separated finish.

5. **Super Suede Bronzer by Saie** — 4 mentions
   Heather Austin and other creators praised this bronzer for its "blurry," naturally radiant finish that adds warmth without looking orange or muddy. It is described as a user-friendly, impossible-to-mess-up formula that bridges the gap between cream and powder.

6. **Dewdrops Palette by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 3 mentions
   Creators lauded this palette for its "otherworldly glow" and intense, smooth shimmers that serve as perfect toppers. Specific shades like *Dew* were singled out for their deceptive shifts—appearing purple in the pan but shifting to intense blue-green on the eyes.

7. **Succulent Dew Palette by SINGE Beauty** — 3 mentions
   Ranked highly by Battybean and utilized by Angelica Nyqvist, this palette is beloved for its unique "grungy garden" color story featuring teals and dusty pinks. Creators appreciate the gradient layout and the high blendability of the matte formula for structural eye looks.

8. **Dead Roses Palette by Bella Beauté Bar** — 3 mentions
   Creators celebrated this palette for its moody, "murky decaying plant" theme, favoring it for grungy, romantic makeup styles. The color story, which blends swampy greens with dusty plum tones, was highlighted for its ability to create depth and unique color contrasts.

9. **Spectra Base by Glisten Cosmetics** — 3 mentions
   Used to create striking, opaque metallic lips and graphic eye elements, this product was noted for its ability to set down fully for long wear. Creators demonstrated its versatility by using shades like *Silver* and *Gold* as both eyeshadow bases and standalone liquid lip colors.

10. **Angelic Palette by Cosmic Brushes** — 2 mentions
    Angelica Nyqvist showcased this palette across both YouTube and Instagram, highlighting the "gorgeous" packaging and high-pigment swatches. It is positioned as a top-tier indie launch, with specific attention drawn to its intricate design and pastel-to-depth color story.
