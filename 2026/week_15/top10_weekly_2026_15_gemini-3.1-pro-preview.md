# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** April 06–12, 2026

1. **Pro Color Pencils by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 14 mentions  
   Creators heavily utilized these pencils for both lip contouring and eye lining, praising their smudge-proof, long-wearing formula. They were frequently featured in complex, multi-product lip combinations, with shades like "Bloodline," "89 Fine," and "Faded" being standout favorites for creating grungy, cool-toned looks.

2. **Spectra Pencils & Speckled Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 14 mentions  
   These versatile pens and pencils were a staple for adding precise, colorful, and freckled details to alternative and editorial makeup looks. Creators frequently used the black and dark blue shades for graphic lip combos, while the taupe and russet speckled pens were widely favored for creating natural-looking faux freckles.

3. **The Little Mermaid Collection by ColourPop** — 8 mentions  
   Driven by 90s nostalgia, creators praised this collaboration for its vibrant, ocean-inspired color story and unique finishes. The multichrome highlighters—specifically the shade "Soft Shell"—were highly favored for providing a bright, ethereal glow, even though one creator noted the formula was slightly flaky.

4. **Eye Primer (Clear & White) by Lethal Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   This primer was consistently praised as an essential base for making pastel and shimmer eyeshadows pop to their fullest potential without altering their true colors. Creators noted its excellent wear time and reliable application, using both the clear and white variants to anchor intricate, colorful eye looks.

5. **Apparition Palette by Gourmande Girls** — 5 mentions  
   Described as the perfect "pastel sister" to the brand's Haunted palette, this release was celebrated for capturing a unique "spooky spring" aesthetic. Creators loved the buttery mattes and high-impact shimmers, noting the palette's versatility in creating both soft pastel and deepened, grungy looks.

6. **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   This mascara was a recurring favorite for completing editorial and glam-grunge makeup looks across Instagram. Creators consistently reached for it to add volume and definition to their lashes, pairing it with both heavy, blown-out smoky eyes and highly reflective shimmer lids.

7. **Glowing Creature of Light by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   Creators utilized this versatile, luminous product primarily as an illuminating primer base for their complexion routines. It was frequently paired with cool-toned, high-contrast makeup looks to provide a dewy, ethereal glow to the skin before foundation application.

8. **Chroma & Glis Glosses by Glisten Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   These glosses were highly sought after for their unconventional shades and multidimensional finishes. Creators specifically highlighted the grey glosses ("Veil" and "Gray Area") for alternative lip looks, as well as the iridescent "Sirius," "Opal," and "Pluto" shades for high-shine, color-shifting lip toppers.

9. **Super Shock Shadows & Highlighters by ColourPop** — 4 mentions  
   A long-standing community favorite, this cream-to-powder formula was praised for its intense, light-reflecting glitter and metallic finishes. Creators showcased shades like "Ripple" and "Ice Dream" as versatile toppers for the eyes, face, and even lips to achieve a wet, glowy aesthetic.

10. **Lab Access Lipsticks by Rituel de Fille** — 4 mentions  
    This new "light as a feather" lipstick formula generated significant excitement, with creators expressing hope that the shades become a permanent offering. The shade "Lilium," described as a neutral shadow peach, was a particular standout that creators often paired with dark lip liners or glosses to create multidimensional lips.
