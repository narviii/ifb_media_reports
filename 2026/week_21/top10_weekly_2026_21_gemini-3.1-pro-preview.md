# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** May 18–24, 2026

1. **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 6 mentions  
   Consistently featured as a holy grail staple in editorial and soft goth routines, creators praise this mascara for delivering dark, defined, and volumized lashes. It was frequently highlighted for its reliability in completing intricate, high-shine eye looks without overpowering them, and was heavily promoted as a gift-with-purchase during the brand's Memorial Day sale.

2. **Intuition Inner Glow Crème Pigment by Rituel de Fille** — 6 mentions  
   Widely utilized as a go-to contour shade, this crème pigment is favored for building ethereal and soft goth aesthetics. Creators applied it to define the cheekbones and jawline, noting that it provides the perfect subtle, cool-toned shadow for moody, high-contrast makeup looks.

3. **Jewels of Eternity Palette by Epic Beauty** — 5 mentions  
   Generating massive excitement for its debut, this palette was celebrated for its "whimsical goth" and "dark fantasy" color story featuring rich jewel tones. Creators were highly impressed by the blendable mattes, celestial multi-dimensional shimmers, and thoughtful details like magnetic pans and romantic illustrated packaging.

4. **Moonlit Creature of Light by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   Frequently used as a luminous base, primer, and highlighter, this product is a favorite for achieving a dewy, ethereal complexion. Creators often mixed it with concealers or applied it directly to the high points of the face to create a natural, light-reflecting glow suited for alternative makeup styles.

5. **Jelly Much Gel Eyeshadow Stick by ColourPop** — 4 mentions  
   Praised for its highly reflective, water-based gel formula, this eyeshadow stick sets quickly and layers easily without fallout. Creators showcased its vibrant pigmentation and "twinkly" sparkle, using it for smooth, direct application to build sunset-inspired, high-shine eye looks.

6. **Taupe Speckled Pen by Glisten Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   Consistently featured across multiple editorial and alternative makeup routines, this pen is a reliable staple for precise facial detailing. Its recurring presence alongside intricate, high-contrast eye designs indicates strong creator trust in its performance and aesthetic versatility.

7. **B12 Base Thinner by ONE/SIZE** — 4 mentions  
   Featured as a complexion-enhancing prep step, this product was specifically highlighted in the "Bronze Pearl" shade. Creators demonstrated its shimmery texture and the radiant, smooth glow it imparts, using it to thin out base products and add a luminous finish to the skin.

8. **Blends Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   Sparking excitement surrounding its highly anticipated restock, this palette was praised for its updated formula and exceptional color payoff. Creators demonstrated its ability to effortlessly build pigment and depth, specifically highlighting the vibrant matte rainbow shades and the newly added high-impact shimmers.

9. **Rose Poetry Eyeshadow Palette by The Pastel Roses** — 3 mentions  
   Admired for its romantic color story, this palette was celebrated for seamlessly bridging the gap between neutral and colorful looks. Creators were highly impressed by the soft, non-staining mattes and the creamy, wet-look shimmers, specifically calling out the shifting blue-purple shade 'Serenade' as a gorgeous standout.

10. **Planet Spirit Palette by Oden's Eye** — 3 mentions  
    A beloved creator collaboration, this palette continues to be a nostalgic favorite for building bold, color-blocked looks. Creators expressed deep affection for the color story, utilizing its vibrant greens and pinks to create structured, sparkly eye designs that stand the test of time.
