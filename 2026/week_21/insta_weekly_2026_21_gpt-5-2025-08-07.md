# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway: 18–24 May 2026 skewed alt and ultra-sparkly: cool-toned “glassy” highlights, icy silvers, and cosmic chromes dominated feeds while soft-goth lips (grey/black plum) and easy embellishments (stars, rhinestones) boosted engagement. Tutorial reels and comparison swatches outperformed static looks.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - High-shine, high-shift week: multichrome eye swatches, wet-versus-dry chrome demos, and “glassy skin” sticks were everywhere.
  - Soft-goth glam threaded through: smoky purples, mossy greens, steel/grey lips, and glitter tears; fast glam via gems/star stamps.
  - Launch/tease energy: Epic Beauty’s Jewels of Eternity rollout, Glisten Bliss 2.0 first looks, Blend Bunny’s Blends restock and “Psilocybin” tease; Kiko’s chromes became the surprise mass favorite.
- Engagement Temperature:
  - Hot. Reels with step-by-step instruction, hacks, or side-by-sides drove saves/shares (“What primer?” “Try it wet!” “Compare to Chanel/Haus Labs?”).
  - Clear purchase intent and FOMO on limited or hard-to-get items (Kiko chromes sold out in some regions; Blend Bunny restock/tease; CheerYep palettes).

- The “Look” Breakdown:
  - Eyes:
    - Dominant textures: high-impact foiled silvers, blue/green duochromes, holo sparkles; wet application for extra metallic payoff.

    - Color stories recurring: icy silver; smoky purple + teal; mossy green + rusty orange (grunge); rainbow strip shimmers for Pride.

  - Cheeks:
    - Cream/liquid stacks; bold orange blush layering; cool blue-violet highlights; experimental blue blush (Make Up For Ever) trending editorially.

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Sparkle-forward singles and toppers:
    - Red shimmer and icy silver multi/duochromes (Terra Moons Vampire Star/Hyades Cluster; Clionadh Myrrh/Orchard/Archway; Dieverune Bismuth; Touch of Glam “Leaf Me Alone”).
  - Soft-goth lip wardrobe:
    - Grey/steel, black-plum, and vampy oxblood bullets and balms (Rituel Black Locust/Lilium, Unearthly Hardware shades, similar to MAC Sin/Film Noir depth).
  - “Glassy” but stable glow:
    - Balmy highlights/eye gloss with low disturbance (Rituel Rare Light, Danessa Vision/Blurring Balm Spray pairings); include glitter adhesives and face-safe gems.
  - Palette bets:
    - Epic Beauty “Jewels of Eternity” (align with regional partners), Terra Moons Pastel Perihelion, CheerYep Ember/Sparkle, Glisten Bliss 2.0; prep waitlists for Blend Bunny’s “Psilocybin.”
- Content Strategy:
  - Short reels that teach:
    - Wet vs dry swatches; side-by-side reformulations; “primer on nose” and “subtle overline” hacks with store SKUs linked.
    - Close-up eye swatches on-iris; ingredient/texture explainers for glassy sticks vs balms.
  - Comparison grids:
    - “If Kiko is sold out, try these”—curate indie dupes by undertone/shift and show wet/dry macros.
  - Community prompts:
    - Celestial stamp/rhinestone mini-challenges (tag-to-win gems/adhesive bundles).
- Gap Analysis:
  - Red shimmer scarcity: curate a “Red Metals Edit” across brands.
  - MAC Film Noir backlash: offer highly pigmented deep maroon/oxblood blushers (powder/cream) and educate on lipstick-as-blush techniques for that tone.
  - Glassy stick base disruption fears: bundle with gripping primers/setting sprays; publish “no-crease glass” application sequence.
  - Peel-off stain sensitivity: provide gentler marker/tint alternatives (transfer-resistant but non-peel).

## 6. Special insights

- Emerging Micro-Trends:
  - Grey/steel lips going mainstream-alt: multiple creators wore grey/black-plum, plus grey gloss demos and “steely” soft-goth looks. Build a dedicated “Steel Lip Bar.”
  - Blue blush and atypical cheek tints: MUFE liquid blues/purples used watercolor-thin on cheeks and nose—expect more editorial color-cheek crossovers.
  - “Minimum effort, maximum slay” adornment: stars, pearls, rhinestones transform soft shimmers into statement looks in minutes—high utility for nightlife and Pride season.
- Unexpected Pairings:
  - High-shift toppers on lips (sparkle sticks/balms dabbed center); icy blue-violet highlighter mirrored at inner corners and bottom lip for cohesive “glow map.”
  - Eye primer repurposed on nose to anchor foundation (MAC Paint Pot), toner-as-grip layer (Numbuzin #9).
- Creative insight:
  - Package an “Alt Sparkle System”: one wet-look topper (icy silver), one colored chrome (blue-green), one gem/stamp tool, one adhesive, and one soft-goth lip—plus a 60-second tutorial template. Tie to Pride and “midnight neon” club looks for immediate seasonal relevance.
