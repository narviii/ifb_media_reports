# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** January 05–11, 2026

1.  **Paris La Boheme Palette by Nomad Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
    This newly announced palette was met with overwhelmingly positive anticipation from creators. It was consistently praised for its unique, destination-inspired color story of purples, grays, and greens, with swatches showcasing pigmented mattes and sparkly, multidimensional shimmers. The palette was frequently described as having "springtime vibes," generating significant buzz ahead of its launch.

2.  **Epiphany Glow Melt On Eyeliner by Kaleidos** — 5 mentions  
    This eyeliner line was repeatedly used by creators to add a vibrant pop of color to the waterline. The formula was praised for its intense pigmentation, long-lasting wear, and non-irritating properties. Creators showcased a variety of shades, including blue, green, and gold, to complete and enhance their eye looks.

3.  **Pinky Promise Palette by Bella Beaute Bar** — 4 mentions  
    This upcoming collaboration palette generated significant buzz through its announcement by creator Dee Dee. The product was positioned to celebrate friendship, with creators expressing strong intent to purchase based on trust in both the collaborator and the brand's highly-rated matte formula. The personal story behind the palette's theme and artwork was a central part of its introduction.

4.  **Cursed Winter Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    The Cursed Winter Palette was a go-to for creators on Instagram looking to create cool-toned eye looks. It was repeatedly featured in looks centered around blue and green shades. The palette was consistently tagged as a core product, indicating its use for building complex and colorful makeup applications.

5.  **SpongeBob Collection Palette by Lethal Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This IP collaboration palette was met with positive anticipation, with creators praising the fun theme and vibrant, jewel-toned color story. Confidence in the launch was high, citing the brand's strong track record with collaborations and recent formula enhancements. The palette was used to create colorful looks and was highlighted as a promising new release.

6.  **Intuition Crème Pigment by Rituel de Fille** — 3 mentions  
    This gray cream pigment was highlighted for its surprising versatility as a makeup tool. Creators demonstrated its utility as a mixing medium to mute and cool down vibrant blushes for a winter-appropriate tone. It was also used as a contour product, proving its value beyond its primary function.

7.  **Dexter Palette by Lethal Cosmetics** — 2 mentions  
    This IP collaboration palette drew mixed but passionate reviews from creators. While one disliked its bulky packaging and chunky shimmers, another lauded it as a favorite, celebrating the thoughtful thematic details and excellent red and smoky color story. The contrasting feedback highlights its appeal to fans of the theme who appreciate the brand's creative execution.

8.  **Archeo Palette by Clionadh Cosmetics** — 2 mentions  
    Creator feedback on this palette was divided based on its formula composition. The shimmers received high praise for being exceptionally beautiful and dimensional, but the matte formula was criticized by one creator for being dusty and difficult to work with, a notable drawback at its premium price point. Despite this, another creator demonstrated successful application of the mattes.

9.  **Ellis Palette (Vol I & II) by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 2 mentions  
    The Ellis palettes were highly regarded as essential neutral matte collections. The Vol. II palette was named a top favorite, celebrated for its "unmatched" matte quality, perfect blendability, and versatility as a staple for daily looks. The original palette was also used for its reliable matte shades, reinforcing the line's reputation for high performance.

10. **Til Death Eyeshadow Palette by Jolié Beauty** — 2 mentions  
    Creators utilized this palette to build the foundation for intense, smoky eye makeup. It was praised for its dark matte shades that provide significant depth and pigmentation. The formula was noted for blending seamlessly, making it a reliable choice for achieving dramatic and structured looks.
