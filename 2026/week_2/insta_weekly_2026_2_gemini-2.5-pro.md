# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

*   **Key Takeaway:** For the week of January 5-11, 2026, the indie beauty landscape was dominated by cool-toned, ethereal, and grunge aesthetics, with a heavy emphasis on high-impact textures like intense shimmers and wet-look glosses. Tutorial-style reels and swatch roundups drove significant engagement, while conversations around custom color mixing and new palette announcements from Bella Beaut√© Bar and Nomad Cosmetics signaled key upcoming trends.

## 1. Overview

*   **The Vibe:** The feed was a dynamic mix of artistry and education. High-engagement video content focused on swatch comparisons (e.g., Smita Holden's "icy blue eyeshadow" roundup) and creative tutorials (e.g., Lindsey Rowley's "bubble makeup"). This was balanced by static, editorial-style images showcasing graphic liner and unconventional lip colors, particularly from creators like s.andromedae. A meta-conversation about the pressures of content creation also emerged, adding a layer of authenticity to the week.

*   **Engagement Temperature:** Audience sentiment was overwhelmingly positive and inquisitive. Comments were less about passive compliments and more about direct product questions ("What eyeshadow is that?"), purchase declarations ("*Adds to basket*"), and community-building (tagging friends). Tutorial videos from creators like Lindsey Rowley and Courtney Quarantine generated high view-to-like ratios, indicating strong audience retention and resonance.

Editorial blue and green metallic lips also made a notable appearance.

## 3. Aesthetic & Hashtag Trends

*   **Visual Themes:** The feed coalesced around a "soft goth" or ethereal grunge aesthetic. Key hashtags included **#gothmakeup**, **#alternativemakeup**, **#graphicliner**, and **#etherealmakeup**. Looks featured diffused, smoky gray eyeshadow, sharp graphic liner, and unconventional lip colors, often paired with dewy, glass-like skin.
*   **Color Stories:**
    *   **Cool-Toned Dominance:** Icy blues, teals, mossy greens, and deep charcoals were the primary color story for eyes.
    *   **Vibrant Pinks & Purples:** A strong secondary trend, particularly in blush and monochromatic eye looks, fueled by the `Huda Beauty` blush palettes and the `Pinky Promise` palette announcement.
    *   **Muted & Desaturated:** The use of gray and taupe as mixers for both blush and lip color points to a sophisticated trend toward custom, muted tones.

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

*   **Stocking Recommendations:**
    *   **Prioritize Cool Tones:** Stock up on eyeshadow palettes and singles featuring icy blues, teals, greens, and moody grays/charcoals.
    *   **Feature "Grunge Lip" Bundles:** Create kits with cool-toned taupe/purple lip liners (dupes for NYX Moonwalk), black pencil liners, and high-shine clear glosses.
    *   **Invest in "Mixer" Pigments:** A gray or taupe cream pigment is a must-have. Market it as a tool for customizing blush and lipstick shades to achieve the trending muted look.
    *   **Anticipate the Pink Wave:** The upcoming **Bella Beaut√© Bar `Pinky Promise`** palette will drive demand for vibrant, deep pinks. Prepare supporting products in this color story.

*   **Content Strategy:**
    *   **"Swatch Roundup" Reels:** This format is a top performer. Create videos comparing multiple brands within a specific color category (e.g., "Our Top 5 Icy Blue Shimmers," "The Best Cool-Toned Nude Liners").
    *   **Tutorials on Micro-Trends:** Create content demonstrating how to use a gray pigment to mute blush or how to achieve the perfect grunge lip. This positions the store as an educational authority.
    *   **Static Editorial Shots:** High-quality, artistic photos of unconventional looks (like graphic lips) perform well for brand building, even with lower direct engagement.

*   **Gap Analysis:**
    *   A user complained about a **ColourPop** Super Shock Shadow being "crumbly with sooo much fallout." This is a clear opportunity to market alternative glitter and shimmer formulas as smoother, more user-friendly, and having less fallout.
    *   The recurring issue of international shipping and availability presents an opportunity to highlight brands with reliable global distribution or to create content around "dupes" for products that are hard to get in certain regions.
