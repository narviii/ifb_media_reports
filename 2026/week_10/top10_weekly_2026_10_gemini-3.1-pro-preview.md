# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** March 02–08, 2026

1. **Woeful Whimsy Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 17 mentions  
   This highly anticipated collaboration with creator @s.andromedae was praised for its unique "cutesy and gloomy" color story, blending staple neutrals with bright pastels. Creators frequently showcased the palette's dimensional shimmers, noting they shift and sparkle beautifully in person. It served as the focal point for numerous vibrant, editorial eye looks and was even repurposed as a blush.

2. **Silver & Cold Palette by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 10 mentions  
   Highlighted as the centerpiece of a new winter-to-spring collection, this palette was favored for its cool-toned color story featuring deep mattes and icy shimmers. Creators noted that the mattes blend easily across diverse skin tones, while the high-impact glitters provide a dramatic, wet-look finish. It was frequently utilized to craft frosty, grunge-inspired editorial aesthetics.

3. **Spring Butterfly Single Eyeshadow Collection by Oden's Eye** — 9 mentions  
   Created in collaboration with Heather Austin, this collection of single shadows was celebrated for its punchy, spring-ready colors and versatile finishes. Analysts noted high praise for the pigmentation of the mattes and the ethereal, reflective sparkle of the multichromes. The collection was frequently used to execute vibrant, nature-inspired looks, including intricate leaf-shaped eye designs.

4. **Old Soul Palette by Glaminatrix Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   Creators appreciated this palette as an elevated take on neutrals, enriched by muted tones like rose, plum, and blue. The buttery soft mattes and spectacular, smooth shimmers received high praise, though one creator noted an overabundance of blue-toned sparkles. It was a favored choice for crafting romantic, soft-goth, and smoky eye looks.

5. **Quicksilver Single Shadow by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This single shadow was frequently featured in creator roundups highlighting the best periwinkle and silver shimmers on the market. Users loved its unique, semi-sheer topper formula that shifts dynamically between silver, periwinkle, and purple. It was highly recommended for achieving an ethereal, high-shine chrome transformation on the lids.

6. **Dead Cute Blush Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This blush palette was frequently utilized alongside vibrant eyeshadows to complete dramatic, alternative makeup looks. Creators relied on the quad to add a complementary, cool-toned flush high on the cheekbones, blending seamlessly with intense highlighter applications. It proved to be a reliable staple for achieving a flushed, editorial aesthetic.

7. **Taupe/Russet Speckled Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   These pens were consistently used by creators to add faux freckles to the skin, enhancing alternative and soft-goth aesthetics. The product provided a reliable, natural-looking speckled effect that paired well with heavy, luminous base makeup. It was frequently layered over full-coverage foundations to reintroduce skin-like texture.

8. **Glimmer Grasp by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This primer was heavily relied upon as an essential tacky base to maximize the impact of chunky shimmers and glitters. Creators frequently applied it to the inner corners and undereyes to ensure high-shine, iridescent shadows adhered perfectly without fallout. It served as a crucial prep step for achieving wet-look, editorial eye designs.

9. **Shiver Gel Flakies by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   These gel flakies were celebrated for adding intense, reflective texture to eye looks without the need for additional adhesives. Creators applied them heavily across the mobile lid and under the eyes for a dramatic, high-glitter, wet-like appearance. They were noted as easy to apply and perfect for high-glamour or festival aesthetics.

10. **Sparkle Veil Mascara Topper by Bella Beaute Bar** — 3 mentions  
    These multichrome mascara toppers were praised for adding a fairy-like, shifty sparkle to both lashes and brows. Creators noted the thin formula dries down completely without flaking, making it effective even over false lashes or bare brows. The shades "Cosmic Chlorophyll" and "Lunar Petal" were highlighted as particular standouts for their vibrant, visible shifts.
