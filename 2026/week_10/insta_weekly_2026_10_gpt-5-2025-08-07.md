# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway: Mar 2–8, 2026 was defined by icy, high-shine sparkle and “whimsy with depth.” Unearthly x s.andromedae’s Woeful Whimsy reveal and Dieverune’s Silver & Cold launch set the tone, while periwinkle, lime, ultraviolet highlight and grungy greens drove shade demand across tutorial reels.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - Launch week energy: multiple reveals, flatlays, and swatch drops (palettes, singles, flakies, mascara toppers).
  - Tutorial-first reels outperformed static: “POV: you’re looking for…” shade editors (periwinkle, lime, mossy/grungy green, ultraviolet highlighter) guided purchasing.
  - Looks skewed editorial: glitter flakies under the eye, holographic toppers, colored mascaras (navy/grey/white), and split-color eyes; lips went glossy vinyl or pearly metallic.

- Engagement Temperature:
  - High excitement and active tagging (“I need this,” “send me this post,” “picking up the palette + singles”).
  - Strong tutorial asks (brow, base, glitter safety/crease control); frequent product comparisons/dupes.
  - Minor friction signals: one shipping/CS complaint on an Unearthly-tagged reveal; restock questions (pastel palettes, prior collabs).

## 2. Product Momentum (Brand & Item Velocity)

- Top Mentioned Brands:
  - Heavy rotation: Unearthly Cosmetics (Woeful Whimsy), Dieverune Cosmetics (Silver & Cold), HALF MAGIC (Glitter Puck/Glitter Pills), Bella Beauté Bar (Sparkle Veil), Oden’s Eye x Heather Austin (Spring Butterfly singles).
  - Supporting players with clear pulls: Time Phoria (gloss), ColourPop (Super Shock), Wet n Wild (Chameleon Chrome), Terra Moons, Clionadh, Violette FR, KVD, Kaleidos, About-Face, Rituel de Fille, Glaminatrix, Melt, J.Cat, Innisfree, mixsoon, Laka.

- Viral Products:
  - Unearthly x s.andromedae Woeful Whimsy (3/12; $48): “neutrals behaving badly” with bright pastels + high-impact shimmers. Commenters called out lime, teal, purple; repeated purchase intent.
  - Dieverune Silver & Cold (3/6): Palette + singles (Quicksilver, Bismuth, Silver Lining), flakies (Shiver/Fluorescence), highlighters (Grimfrost/Frostbow), colored mascaras. Multiple creators: “diamond-like sparkle,” good on deep skin; clear “I need to try the brand.”
  - HALF MAGIC Glitter Puck “Princess Ghost” + Magic Drip “Virtual BFF”: ubiquitous lower-lash sparkle demos; eye-safety questions appeared and were addressed (ophthalmologist-tested callouts).
  - Bella Beauté Bar Sparkle Veil mascara toppers: visible on bare lashes and brows; comments: “How do I add to cart,” “need green/dark purple.”
  - Time Phoria Lunara Frost 3D Gloss (Odyssey, Dunara): “vinyl-shine, non-sticky”; high views/likes; “living in my everyday bag.”
  - Budget breakouts: Wet n Wild Chameleon Chrome singles drove “wet dream” reactions; ColourPop Super Shock (Rooftop Cocktails, Dream Much) remained the affordable anchor.
  - Skincare x base with momentum: Innisfree SPF 50 (no pilling, moisturizer feel) and mixsoon hydrogel mask (visible smoothing) elicited “I need this.”

- The “Look” Breakdown:
  - EYES:
    - Mattes: lime/chartreuse, teal/cyan, periwinkle/purple, cool greys/black to scaffold shimmer.
    - Toppers/Textures: multichrome/duochrome glitter, gel flakies, holographic singles; ultraviolet/icy blue highlights used as inner-corner/brow bone.
    - Lashes: navy, grey, white mascaras and sparkle toppers on lashes and brows.
  - LIPS:
    - High-gloss, non-sticky, slightly plumping (Time Phoria; HALF MAGIC Dreamie Genie; About-Face Cherry Pick).
    - Moody mixers: Unearthly “Lore” as a cool-down mixer; blue/grey lip combos; pearlized mauves (Kryolan 653).
    - Oil/gel tints (Rituel de Fille Night Thorn; Laka gloss/tints) for juicy shine.
  - CHEEKS/HIGHLIGHT:
    - Silver/blue-shift, “UV” highlighters (Dieverune Frostbow; Clionadh Myrrh/Tourmaline Tears comps; BBB Dark Dreamscape).
    - Cream/liquid blush present; note: Benefit Play Days blushes perceived sheer/expensive vs payoff.

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Sparkle toolkit: ophthalmologist-tested glitters/gel flakies, multichrome toppers (Dieverune/Clionadh/Terra Moons), UV/icy highlighters, and periwinkle/lime/mossy-green shimmers (budget and indie tiers).
  - Colored lash wardrobe: navy/grey/white mascaras plus glitter toppers (BBB Sparkle Veil; Dieverune colored mascaras).
  - Gloss renaissance: non-sticky, high-shine plumping glosses (Time Phoria analogs; HALF MAGIC Dreamie Genie); pearly lipsticks (Kryolan-style) and moody mixers (cool-down nudes).
  - Pastel-with-depth palettes: Woeful Whimsy; cool-toned shimmer-forward (Dieverune Silver & Cold); Spring Butterfly singles (fill shade gaps at low AUR).

- Content Strategy:
  - Replicate high-performing “POV shade finder” reels (periwinkle, lime, grungy green, ultraviolet) with arm + eye swatches on multiple skintones.
  - Glitter safety and placement mini-guides (lower-lash sparkle, brow toppers) to convert the “afraid to use near eye” crowd.
  - Short lip-combo transformations (liner + blur matte + mixer) with shade codes on-screen.
  - “Under $10 sparkles” series (Wet n Wild/ColourPop/J.Cat) to capture value-seekers.

- Gap Analysis:
  - Sheer/liquid blush frustration at price-to-pigment (Benefit): offer higher-payoff liquids/creams or bundle demos showing build.
  - Creasing anxiety: publish setting-layering guides (Robert Welsh style) and eye-base + emollient control tips.
  - Shipping/CS sensitivity during launch spikes: emphasize fast dispatch, clear ETAs, and proactive comms.

## 6. Special insights

- Emerging Micro-Trends:
  - Mascara toppers as brows: glitter brows are back (BBB Sparkle Veil used on brows widely without flaking).
  - Ultraviolet as face-highlight category: multiple indie options, audience actively seeking shade recs and side-by-sides.
  - Lime is back: matte lime considered “hard to come by” and heavily desired; paired with teal/purple or grey bases.

- Unexpected Pairings:
  - Eyeliner as lip liner to sculpt goth gradients (Flower Knows demo); eyeshadows used on lips (s.andromedae).
  - Glitter pucks on chest/tattoos; glitter freckles with flakies for festival editorials.

- Creative insight:
  - Package a “Whimsigoth Sparkle Edit” kit: lime matte single + periwinkle/UV topper + navy mascara + eye-safe flakies + moody gloss. Include a QR to 3 quick looks (neutral-with-secret, candy-carnival pop, soft-goth smoke).
  - Launch “Shade Concierge” stories weekly: crowdsource niche color hunts (e.g., true blood-red iridescent) to own the discovery moment and drive cross-brand baskets.
