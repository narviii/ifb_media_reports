# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** January 01–04, 2026

1.  **Single Shadows by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
    Creators consistently used these single eyeshadows for high-impact, sparkly, and textured eye looks. Specific shades like 'Garland' and 'Christmas Lights' were featured in festive tutorials, while collaboration shades were showcased in creator looks. The singles were frequently praised across videos for being "so pretty," "so impactful," and delivering significant luminosity and dimension.

2.  **Colorfix Paint by Danessa Myricks Beauty** — 4 mentions  
    This multi-use pigment, particularly in the shade 'lift,' was repeatedly featured in "Get Ready With Me" videos as a foundational base product. Creators applied it to brighten the under-eye area and prep the face before other complexion products. Its consistent use suggests it is a staple for achieving a perfected and lifted base.

3.  **Lip Glosses by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    Creators highlighted multiple lip gloss formulas from the brand, noting positive experiences with both opaque and sheer finishes. The 'Lip Glazes' were praised for being non-sticky and highly pigmented, while the 'Lip Oil Glosses' were favored for their comfortable, sheer, and sparkly wet-look shine.

4.  **Celestial Sphere Gelée Eye Gloss by Rituel de Fille** — 3 mentions  
    This product was described as perfect for achieving a glossy, "lived-in" or "cool girl" aesthetic on the eyes. Creators used shades like 'Crux' and 'Pyxis' to add an iridescent shimmer and a wet-look sheen to their makeup. The sentiment was consistently positive, focusing on its unique finish and ethereal effect.

5.  **Glitterpuck Pressed Glitter by Half Magic** — 3 mentions  
    This pressed glitter was highly praised for its dense, opaque glitter payoff that creates an intense "wet" look. Creators recommended shades like 'Glimmer Pop' and 'Cyber Diamond' for their versatility, using them as an eye topper, a face highlighter, or a standalone true silver shadow.

6.  **Lip Pencils by Black Moon Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    Ranked as a top favorite by one creator, these lip pencils were lauded for their smooth, creamy, and pigmented formula that sets down and lasts. The unique "grungey cool-toned shades" like 'Premonition' and 'Casket' were highlighted for creating defined ombré lips and filling a needed gap in makeup collections.

7.  **Merry Chrissy Palette by Glaminatrix Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This palette received positive attention for its unique Australian Christmas theme and "gorgeous" shimmer formulas. Creators showcased its high pigmentation and dimensional shine through arm swatches and praised the fun and impressive mix of traditional holiday colors with warm, sandy tones.

8.  **Boucle Silk Airbrushed Talc-Free Finishing Powder by ONE/SIZE by Patrick Starrr** — 3 mentions  
    Mentioned as a favorite in a "best of the year" recap and used in multiple GRWMs, this powder was praised for its ability to act as a "filter" over makeup. Creators noted it helps achieve a healthy, luminous, and perfected skin look without appearing heavy or glittery.

9.  **Ash & Ember Eye Soot by Rituel de Fille** — 2 mentions  
    This product was used to create simple and effective grungy eye looks. Creators highlighted its unique powdery texture that delivers an intense, satiny finish without high shine. The deep shade 'Obsidian' was specifically mentioned for achieving a dramatic, smoky effect.

10. **Colorfix Stix by Danessa Myricks Beauty** — 2 mentions  
    Creators praised these eyeshadow sticks as a favorite for achieving "super sparkly, super intense" results with minimal effort. The innovative magnetic, dual-ended packaging and easy-to-use metallic formula were noted as ideal for creating quick and impactful eye looks, especially for travel.
