# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** March 23–29, 2026

1. **Apparition Palette by Gourmande Girls** — 8 mentions  
   Creators praised the palette's "Spooky Spring" and "pastel-goth" aesthetic, noting its highly unique color story. Reviews consistently highlighted the intense pigmentation of the dark mattes and the beautiful, wet-looking shifts of the marbled and duochrome shimmers. It was frequently used to create cohesive cool-toned and pastel galaxy looks.

2. **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 7 mentions  
   This mascara emerged as a staple in one creator's routine, appearing consistently across multiple dramatic, editorial eye looks. It was repeatedly used to add volume and length to both upper and lower lashes, serving to ground highly pigmented and glitter-heavy eyeshadow applications.

3. **Audacious Collection by Shine By SD Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   Creators showcased these highly pigmented, sparkling single eyeshadows as a beautiful, vibrant option for spring. The formula was praised for its intense shimmer and color-shifting duochrome properties, with shades like "Untamed Aura" and "Edge of Glow" frequently used to highlight the lid and inner corners.

4. **Intuition Contour by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   This contour product was consistently utilized to add soft, cool-toned sculpting to the face. It was favored for providing subtle, natural-looking definition that complemented elaborate, brightly colored, and graphic makeup looks without overpowering them.

5. **Taupe/Russet Speckled Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   These pens were utilized regularly to add faux freckles and subtle texture to the skin. Creators paired this natural-looking freckle application with luminous bases and maximalist eye makeup for a contrasting, editorial aesthetic.

6. **Glowing Creature of Light by Rituel de Fille** — 4 mentions  
   Used interchangeably as a luminous makeup primer and a serum illuminator, this product was praised for giving the skin a dewy, highlighted finish. Creators noted it provided a seamless, glowing base that paired beautifully with both soft makeup and heavy glitter applications.

7. **Lavender Fields Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   Reviewers were highly impressed by the sophisticated mix of lavender and muted green tones, describing the color story as a beautiful take on spring. The palette received positive feedback for its pigmented pastel mattes, glossy shimmers, and luxurious packaging.

8. **Succulent Dew Palette by SINGE Beauty** — 3 mentions  
   Creators highlighted this palette for its botanical-themed teal and berry tones, praising its beautifully executed mix of colorful and dark shades. It was described as a standout favorite from the brand, perfect for creating ethereal and visually striking looks.

9. **Woeful Whimsy Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   This palette was celebrated for its great combination of whimsical and melancholic shades. Notably, one creator creatively repurposed the bright pink, green, blue, and purple eyeshadow pans as vibrant, unconventional blushes, praising the formula's blendability on the cheeks.

10. **Princess Ghost Glitterpuck by Half Magic Beauty** — 3 mentions  
    Creators were enthusiastic about this product's dazzling, icy blue sparkles that softly shift to indigo. It was highly praised for its high-shine, iridescent finish and ease of application, frequently used to add ethereal sparkle to the eyes and cheekbones.
