# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** March 23–29, 2026

1. **Apparition Palette by Gourmande Girls** — 9 mentions  
   Creators highly praised the "spooky spring" and pastel-goth aesthetic of this palette. The formula was celebrated for its highly pigmented dark mattes, beautiful multichrome shimmers, and unique marbled shades that transform on the eyes. It was frequently used to create contrasting, ethereal, and grungy pastel looks.

2. **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 9 mentions  
   This mascara was a staple in alternative and graphic makeup routines, frequently used by creators to finish off highly editorial, glitter-heavy eye looks. It consistently provided the necessary definition to stand out against intense, multi-dimensional eyeshadows and sharp graphic liners.

3. **Audacious Collection by Shine By SD Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
   Creators were extremely enthusiastic about these single shadows, highlighting their vibrant, colorful, and color-shifting properties. The collection was frequently used to add intense, high-shine multichrome and duochrome metallic finishes to the lids and inner corners, with shades like "Untamed Aura" and "Edge of Glow" being standout favorites.

4. **Intuition for Contour by Rituel de Fille** — 6 mentions  
   This product served as a go-to contouring staple for creators executing alternative, goth, and editorial makeup looks. It was consistently relied upon to provide soft, cool-toned sculpting that complemented dramatic eye makeup and luminous skin finishes.

5. **Taupe/Russet Speckled Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   Creators utilized these speckled pens primarily to add faux freckles and enhance the skin's natural texture. They were a recurring favorite for adding a playful, lived-in element to highly stylized, graphic, and glitter-heavy makeup applications.

6. **Creature of Light Serum Illuminator by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   Often used as a luminous primer or base, this illuminator was praised for giving the skin a dewy, ethereal glow. Creators layered it under their base makeup to achieve a highly reflective, radiant finish that paired perfectly with heavy glitter and alternative aesthetics.

7. **The Little Mermaid Collection by ColourPop** — 4 mentions  
   Creators expressed strong nostalgia and excitement for this Disney collaboration, particularly praising the brand's new chrome highlighter formula for its beautiful color shifts. The collection's eyeshadow palette was also noted for its approachable mix of neutrals and punchy, duochrome shimmers without the inclusion of pressed glitters.

8. **Glitterpuck by Half Magic** — 4 mentions  
   Highlighted for its dazzling, icy sparkle, this pressed product (especially the shade "Princess Ghost") was a favorite for adding ethereal, high-shine dimension to looks. Creators loved its ease of application and iridescent finish, frequently tapping it onto the cheeks, lids, and brow bone for a magical effect.

9. **Precision Sculpture Stylo by KJH Brand** — 4 mentions  
   Creators loved the creamy, blendable, cream-to-powder formula of these stylos, using them versatilely across the face. They were specifically favored as soft, smoky lip liners to create moody, diffused lip combos, as well as for adding depth to the lower lash line.

10. **Lavender Fields Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This palette was highly recommended for spring looks due to its sophisticated, muted mix of lavender and green tones. Creators were impressed by the creamy, pigmented pastel mattes, the glossy shimmers, and the overall luxurious feel of the packaging.
