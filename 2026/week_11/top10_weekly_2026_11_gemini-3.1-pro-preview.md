# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** March 09–15, 2026

1. **Woeful Whimsy Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 18 mentions  
   Highly anticipated as a collaboration with creator s.andromedae, this palette dominated weekly discussions for its mix of staple neutrals and vibrant pastels. Creators praised the smooth, non-dusty matte formula—particularly the neon green "Gumdrop"—and highlighted the highly reflective, "twinkly" shimmers. The palette was consistently used to create soft, whimsical, and grungy looks, receiving overwhelmingly positive sentiment.

2. **Cleric Palette by Fantasy Cosmetica** — 11 mentions  
   Creators expressed genuine enthusiasm for this RPG-inspired palette, frequently highlighting its cohesive, grungy color story featuring greens, blues, and neutrals. The intense, reflective quality of the shimmers and the smooth, highly pigmented mattes were frequently praised. Many creators presented it in highly aesthetic, thematic flatlays and called it a new favorite from the brand.

3. **Midnight in Aspen Palette by Touch of Glam** — 10 mentions  
   This magnetic palette was celebrated for its high-impact, complex multichrome and holographic shimmers that provide an ethereal, wintery glow. While the specialty shimmers were universally loved for their intense payoff, some creators felt the matte formula was powdery or sheer. This resulted in mixed reviews for the mattes but overwhelmingly positive endorsements for the palette's unique, sparkly finishes.

4. **Lavender Fields Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
   Creators loved the thematic split of earthy tones and vibrant purples, noting it perfectly captures the aesthetic of a natural lavender field. The matte formula was highlighted as the brand's best yet—smooth, soft, and highly pigmented—while the loosely pressed shimmers provided intense, multi-dimensional sparkle. Reviewers specifically appreciated that the shimmer formula felt soft without being prone to hard-panning.

5. **Spring Butterfly Single Eyeshadow Collection by Oden's Eye** — 4 mentions  
   A collaboration with Heather Austin, this collection of 15 single shades was praised for its vibrant, whimsical aesthetic and affordable bundle pricing. Creators specifically highlighted the high pigmentation of the matte brights and the watery, fine shimmer of the metallic shades. The unique butterfly-themed packaging and individual pan names were also noted as standout details.

6. **Swamp Goddess by Half Magic** — 4 mentions  
   Frequently featured in creator roundups of the best green and brown duochromes, this shade was praised as a classic, vibrant yellowish-green shimmer. Creators highlighted its strong reflective quality and metallic finish, making it a go-to single shadow. It was consistently recommended for those looking to create grungy, ethereal eye looks.

7. **Spectra Lash Mascara by Glisten Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This colored mascara was used to add playful, vibrant details to whimsical makeup looks, particularly in shades like "Rose Red" and "Sky Blue." Creators appreciated its ability to match colorful hair, outfits, and graphic eyeliner. It was emphasized as a fun, high-impact tool for creative self-expression and maximum "whimsy."

8. **Masterclass Collection by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   A collaboration with makeup artist Jordan Liberty, this collection of neutral colored pro pencils was highly praised for its inclusive shade range and budge-proof formula. Creators loved the versatility of the pencils, noting they perform exceptionally well as lip liners, lipsticks, or eye bases across various skin tones. The formula's extreme longevity and water resistance were highlighted as major selling points.

9. **Starr Gazing Palette by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   Created in collaboration with Ellie Starr, this palette was celebrated for its deep space and Northern Lights-inspired color story. Creators highlighted the unique, chunky glitter formulas and deep, vibrant mattes, praising the cohesive, celestial aesthetic of the entire collection. The customized packaging and highly reflective finishes made it a standout for indie shimmer enthusiasts.

10. **Miss Matrix Liner by Kaleidos Makeup** — 3 mentions  
    This eyeliner was consistently used by creators to add complementary cool tones or dark definition to the waterline. It was favored for its ability to anchor bright, whimsical eye looks and pair seamlessly with vibrant indie eyeshadow palettes. The liner served as a reliable staple for completing complex, colorful tutorials.
