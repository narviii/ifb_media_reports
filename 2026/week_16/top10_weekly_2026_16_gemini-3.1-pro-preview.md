# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** April 13–19, 2026

1. **Yin & Yang Palette by Bella Beaute Bar** — 7 mentions  
   Creators praised this collaboration palette for its unique, cool-toned aesthetic and beautifully curated grey color story. Reviewers highlighted the high-quality, sparkly shimmers and holographic shades, noting it is perfect for creating soft, grungy looks. Sentiment was overwhelmingly positive, with even those who typically avoid grey tones expressing admiration for the palette's execution.

2. **Apparition Palette by Gourmande Girls** — 4 mentions  
   This palette garnered attention for its unique color story that successfully pairs soft pastels with deep, grungy mattes and moody blues. While sentiment was largely positive regarding the aesthetic and blendability, it received mixed feedback from one creator who experienced lifting with a pastel pink shade. Overall, it was highly anticipated and praised for its versatile, moody vibe.

3. **Pastel Perihelion Collection by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   Creators were thrilled with this capsule collection, frequently highlighting the stunning contrast between its muted pastel mattes and highly reflective chrome shimmers. Standout shades like the minty-green "Nimbus" and the multi-colored "Between Stars" were heavily praised for their ethereal, sparkly shifts. The collection received glowing reviews for its smooth formulas and high-impact finishes.

4. **Chill Blush by Melt Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This powder blush was highly recommended for its beautiful "nudey-rosy" and pinky-brown mauve tones that blend effortlessly into the skin. Creators noted it as a reliable, everyday staple that layers well and provides a soft, flattering flush. The sentiment was universally positive, with users praising both the shade selection and the comfortable formula.

5. **Spectra Pencil in Black by Glisten Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This black pencil was frequently utilized by creators to anchor and add depth to bold, alternative lip looks. Reviewers applied it to the outer edges of the lips to create striking ombre effects and ground bright, duochrome glosses. It was highlighted as an essential, everyday staple for those who favor soft goth and grunge aesthetics.

6. **Starr Gazing Palette by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   This palette was recognized for its intensely colorful and vibrant shimmer story, making it a favorite for dramatic, editorial eye looks. While one creator felt the bright colors didn't align with her neutral preferences, others praised its high-impact pigments and ethereal shifts. It was frequently paired with other indie palettes to create complex, multidimensional makeup art.

7. **Evergreens Petite Stack by Melt Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   Creators expressed high enthusiasm for this 420-themed green palette, specifically praising its "murky" and grungy color story. The olive, chartreuse, and deep green shades were highlighted for their intense pigmentation and smooth blendability. Sentiment was highly positive, with long-time fans of the brand thrilled by the cohesive and thematic shade selection.

8. **Trixie Slix by Trixie Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   This gloss stick received rave reviews for its incredibly comfortable, gel-like formula that feels hydrating and cushiony on the lips. Creators described the texture as "divine" and "unreal," noting that it wears beautifully without feeling sticky. The product was frequently paired with the brand's lip liners to create plush, everyday lip combos.

9. **Lip Glaze by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   This lip glaze was featured as a crucial component in a creator's go-to, long-lasting lip combination. It was praised for its ability to cool down warmer lip stains and add a comfortable, pouty finish without feeling heavy. The product proved to be a reliable daily staple for achieving a glossy, dimensional nude lip.

10. **Staining Lip Liner by Kulfi Beauty** — 3 mentions  
    Creators hailed this product as a game-changer due to its unique pencil application that leaves behind a durable, long-lasting stain. The shade "La La La" was specifically highlighted as a perfect cool-toned pinky nude that comfortably survives all-day wear. It was heavily relied upon as the foundational step for bulletproof, everyday lip combos.
