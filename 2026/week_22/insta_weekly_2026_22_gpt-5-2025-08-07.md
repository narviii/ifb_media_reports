## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Priority palettes: Singe Beauty The Huldra (+ Half Lashes), Fantasy Cosmetica x Multichromatica Cosmic Cove, Blend Bunny Psilocybin and updated Blends (with shimmers), Nomad Electric Carnival (launch 6/1; manage expectations for emollient formula with primer add-ons).
  - Cool-metal lip/eye: Unearthly Hardware (Concrete, Chrome, Gunmetal, Mercury); chrome lip toppers (silver/blue).
  - Singles/topper ecosystem: Clionadh Glow Quad or similar sheer, no-white-base chromes; red-leaning multichrome singles (Terra Moons, Clionadh, Shine by SD).
  - Tools/liners: Glisten Spectra pens (true red); half lashes (Singe) for “whimsigoth” looks.
  - Mass-velocity add-on: ColourPop Jelly Much Sticks (Happy Tears, Aura Points) for inner-corner/brow-bone sparkle shoppers.
- Content Strategy:
  - Double down on eye-swatch reels + short tutorials (placement tips, periwinkle+orange, graphic red liner over shadow). These outperformed static images.
  - Compare/contrast reels: Cosmic Cove vs Electric Carnival vs Psilocybin shimmers; daylight vs UV; finger vs brush pickup.
  - Problem-solving shorts: “How to stop emollient multichromes from creasing (bases, setting, lid prep),” “Best primers for textured shimmers/jelly sticks,” “Brown liner + taupe glaze on deeper skin.”
- Gap Analysis:
  - Red liquid liner scarcity (opaque over shadow) — expand true-red options.
  - Emollient shimmer anxiety (hooded lids) — bundle shimmer bases (Dede Signature Lock-in, NYX Glitter Primer, About-Face Shadow Fix) and education.
  - Hypoallergenic/eye-safe glitter questions — add clear labeling/FAQ and curate “eye-safe sparkle” filters.
  - Magnetic pan interest — call out pan format clearly (magnetic/non) and offer empty magnet palettes.
