# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** May 25–31, 2026

1. **The Huldra Palette by SINGE Beauty** — 21 mentions  
   Created by Angelica Nyqvist, this highly anticipated 20-pan palette is inspired by Swedish folklore. Creators praised its cohesive, grungy forest-inspired color story featuring deep mossy greens, rich earthy browns, and high-impact multichromes. Reviews highlighted the exceptional blendability of the mattes and the wet-look, reflective shine of the shimmer formulas.

2. **Half Lashes by SINGE Beauty** — 19 mentions  
   Launched alongside The Huldra palette, these half lashes (in styles like Stealth Wealth, Whimsigoth, and Office Siren) were widely praised for their comfort and ease of use. Creators noted that they provide a beautiful, lifted outer-corner effect without the heaviness of a full strip lash. They were frequently used to complement both natural and dramatic alternative eye looks.

3. **Cosmic Cove Palette by Fantasy Cosmetica** — 14 mentions  
   A collaboration with creator Multichromatica, this palette was celebrated for its vibrant, deep-sea aesthetic. Creators loved the high-contrast mix of saturated neon mattes paired with spectacular, shifting multichromes. The formula was commended for its buttery pigmentation and ability to effortlessly create moody, grungy oceanic looks.

4. **Hardware Collection by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 10 mentions  
   This industrial-themed collection features cool-toned lipsticks and liquid eyeshadows in shades of grey, silver, and taupe. Creators were highly impressed by the "Mercury" liquid shadow's lightweight, water-based formula and intense scattered sparkle. The collection's lip products, particularly the "Concrete" shade, were favored for anchoring alternative, grungy makeup aesthetics.

5. **Electric Carnival Palette by Nomad Cosmetics** — 10 mentions  
   An all-multichrome palette that excited creators with its intense, high-impact reflectivity and vibrant festival-inspired colors. While the silky, buttery formula delivered spectacular color shifts and foiled finishes, some creators noted the emollient texture might require a primer to prevent creasing. It was highly recommended for users seeking bold, one-and-done sparkly eye looks.

6. **Psilocybin Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
   Praised for its whimsical, fungal-themed color story, this palette successfully pairs grounded earthy neutrals with bright, psychedelic pops of color. Creators highlighted Blend Bunny’s consistently flawless matte formula and appreciated the addition of high-shine shimmers. It was frequently used to create fun, vibrant, and highly blended artistic looks.

7. **Jelly Much Shadow Sticks by ColourPop** — 7 mentions  
   These new shadow sticks were well-received as an affordable, high-impact option for adding intense sparkle to eye looks. Creators appreciated the unique shade offerings—especially the iridescent and baby blue tones—and noted the formula's smooth, foiled finish. They were commonly used as inner corner highlights or quick, all-over lid shimmers.

8. **Spectra / Speckled Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
   These dual-ended eyeliner pens were praised for their precision and the fun, whimsical touch provided by their built-in stamps. Creators highlighted the impressive opacity of the colors, noting that even shades like red showed up vibrantly over dark eyeshadow bases. They were a staple for adding creative, graphic details to alternative makeup looks.

9. **Chrome Book Vol. 3 by Dede's Signature** — 6 mentions  
   This 15-pan palette stood out for its innovative split-pan shimmers and intensely reflective pressed pigments. Creators lauded the extreme sparkle, multidimensional shifts, and the high-contrast pairing of earthy mattes with vibrant jewel-toned metallics. It was highly recommended for shimmer enthusiasts looking for effortless, high-impact shine.

10. **Yin & Yang Palette by Bellabeautebar** — 6 mentions  
    A collaboration with creator freya86_ff, this palette was celebrated for its moody, gothic aesthetic and cool-toned monochromatic color story. Creators loved the mix of smooth greys and blacks with complex, chunky multichrome glitters. It was frequently used to create dramatic, editorial dark-fantasy and "whimsigoth" makeup looks.
