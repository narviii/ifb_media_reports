# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** March 16–22, 2026

1. **Starr Gazing Palette by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
   Creators praised this highly anticipated collaboration with Ellie Starr, highlighting its blue-green aurora-inspired color story. The palette was frequently used to create dramatic, galactic halo eyes, with users specifically commending the intense, multi-dimensional shimmers and electric lime green mattes.

2. **Glowing Creature of Light by Rituel de Fille** — 7 mentions  
   This product was consistently utilized as a luminous primer base across multiple highly detailed, alternative makeup looks. It provided a dewy, radiant finish that perfectly complemented heavy, cool-toned eye makeup and striking, colorful aesthetics.

3. **Intuition by Rituel de Fille** — 7 mentions  
   Frequently paired with the brand's luminous primer, this product served as a staple for soft, cool-toned contouring. Creators utilized it to sculpt the cheekbones subtly without overpowering the vibrant, multi-tonal eye and lip colors featured in their editorial looks.

4. **Speckled Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   Creators heavily relied on these pens, particularly in the shades "Taupe" and "Russet," to add intricate details like faux freckles to their makeup routines. The pens were praised for their ability to add a natural, textured element to highly stylized, colorful, and grunge-inspired looks.

5. **Masterclass Structure Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   Created in collaboration with Jordan Liberty, these face palettes were celebrated for their inclusive, day-one launch catering to light, medium, and deep skin tones. Creators loved the pro-approved, fully magnetic design and demonstrated their versatility by using the contour and highlight shades to create beautifully blended, neutral eye looks.

6. **Woeful Whimsy Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   This creator collaboration palette was frequently reached for to build vibrant, colorful matte bases for intricate eye looks. Users showcased its blendability by creating striking gradients with its grungy purples, bright lime greens, and vivid teals.

7. **Spring Butterfly Single Eyeshadow Collection by Oden's Eye** — 3 mentions  
   This collaboration with Heather Austin was highlighted for its stunning multichrome and shimmer finishes, particularly the shade "Forest Echo." Creators showcased the singles in bright, spring-ready eye looks, noting the formula's high-impact sparkle and flawless execution.

8. **Silver & Cold Palette by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   Creators praised this palette for its inspiring cool-toned color story and high-impact shimmers. It was used to create dramatic, icy looks featuring intense silver metallic lids blended into deep, smoky gray and navy outer corners.

9. **Lavender Fields Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 2 mentions  
   This palette was highlighted for its beautiful mix of earthy tones, berry shades, and highly reflective shimmers. Creators demonstrated its versatility and high shimmer payoff, noting how seamlessly the unique color story blends together.

10. **Glass Load Lip Melts by Kaleidos** — 2 mentions  
    These lip melts received rave reviews for their incredibly shiny, frosty finish that feels plush and hydrating without any stickiness. Creators specifically loved the shade "Cipher" for its cyberpunk, fairy-dust aesthetic and innovative refillable packaging.
