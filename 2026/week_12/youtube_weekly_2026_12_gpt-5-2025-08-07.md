# Weekly YouTube Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key takeaway: Week of 2026-03-16 to 2026-03-22 was pastel-forward with an alt twist. Indie palettes (lavender/green and olive/green families) and high-shine toppers dominated; adhesive eye bases and deep vamp lips served as the counterpoint.

## 1. Overview

- The week skewed spring but not saccharine: creators paired pastel mattes (lavender, sage, periwinkle) with metallic/multichrome lids and grounded the looks with moody lips. “Fairy-grunge” energy persisted.
- There was notable palette enthusiasm rather than complexion discourse. Where base came up, it was about sheering/mixing rather than full-coverage debates (e.g., base thinners + tints).
- Adhesives and eye bases were ubiquitous in tutorials to maximize shimmer payoff; creators named the specific products (Shimmer Stay, Sand Seal, Sigma base), signaling process-driven purchasing.
- Cross-creator visibility spiked around several indie launches/collections: Simply Posh Lavender Fields, Glaminatrix The Old Soul, Oden’s Eye (Stone & Rock; collab singles), Lethal x SpongeBob. Adept Sapphire Selene is entering the hype pipeline via unboxings.
- Shorts pushed niche lip shades (blackened purples, taupey browns) and liner+liquid combos, reinforcing alt aesthetics alongside spring eyes.

## 3. Category Trends

- Momentum categories
  - Eyeshadow palettes: Pastels (lavender/periwinkle) and greens (sage/olive) led.
  - Eye primers/adhesives: Named, repeated usage to enable high-shine/foil lids.
  - Lips: Blackened plum, sepia-brown, and taupe combos; matte/liquid textures favored.
- Textures and aesthetics
  - High-shine metallics/multichromes on lids; soft, diffused pastel mattes in crease.
  - “Fairy-grunge”: ethereal color stories paired with moody liners/lips.
- Seasonality
  - Clear spring shift toward pastels and greens; however, creators resist full soft-glam by anchoring looks with alt lips and graphic liners.

## 4. Emerging Themes & Narratives

- Pastel-green renaissance in indie palettes (cross-creator): Simply Posh Lavender Fields; Glaminatrix The Old Soul; Oden’s Eye greens; multiple creators centered green/lavender stories.
- Adhesives and eye bases as must-have companions: Shimmer Stay (Simply Posh, Bella Beauté Bar), DeDe Sand Seal, Sigma Ignite—creators emphasize process and tools for payoff.
- Collaborations/collections drive attention and cross-video mentions: Oden’s Eye x Heather singles; Lethal x SpongeBob; “New Indie” roundups augment discovery and comparison shopping.
- Preorder/unboxing pipeline fuels hype: Simply Posh (preorder); Adept Sapphire Selene (unboxing) indicate near-term demand spikes and possible stock volatility.
- Western indie momentum remains strong (US/UK/AU, Nordic-owned Oden’s Eye), with consistent creator codes and layered brand mixing in single looks.

## 5. Purchase-Intent Signals

- High-intent triggers
  - Demonstrated shimmer payoff with adhesives (before/after effect), seamless pastel blending, and unique lavender/green color pairings.
  - “Available for preorder” calls and affiliate codes tied to Simply Posh; repeated appearances within the same week.
  - Repeat exposure to Glaminatrix The Old Soul across creators (feature video + worn in GRWM) suggests add-to-cart behavior from viewers seeking moody neutrals compatible with spring greens.
  - Adept Sapphire Selene unboxing primes early adopters; Adept’s history of quick sellouts elevates urgency.
  - Shorts repeatedly spotlighting specific Black Moon lip shades build shade-level demand.
- Value framing
  - Mixing mediums/base thinners pitched as extending a base product’s utility (cost-per-use upgrade).
  - Eye bases/adhesives positioned as small-ticket add-ons that unlock “editorial” results with existing palettes.

- Merchandising and bundles
  - “Fairy-Grunge Pastel Kit”: lavender/green palette + shimmer adhesive + deep plum liquid lip + colored liner.
  - “Pastel Engineered Shine” add-on: adhesive + eye base + flat shader brush; optimize AOV with education.
- Content and timing
  - Move quickly on Adept Sapphire Selene (or create a waitlist) to capture early hype; communicate restock expectations.
  - Feature creator-informed tutorials (sage-to-lavender gradients; periwinkle inner corner) to mirror trending techniques.
- Gaps to exploit

  - Periwinkle and olive single shadows (matte and topper) for customers who want to extend existing palettes without buying full new ones.
  - Base mixers/thinners category: add an indie-friendly option and education on sheering thicker bases.
- De-prioritize
