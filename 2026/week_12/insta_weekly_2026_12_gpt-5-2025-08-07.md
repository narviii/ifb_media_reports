# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway (Mar 16–22, 2026): Blue–green “aurora” eyes with lime flashes, holographic toppers, and alt/goth glossy lips dominated; Terra Moons x Ellie Starr drove the week’s sparkle while Blend Bunny’s Masterclass V2 cemented pro-grade neutrals and lip glazes as everyday workhorses.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - Heavy on swatches, macro sparkles, and quick tutorials; creators mixed “education-first” reels (placement, layering) with high-impact lip combos.
  - Launch-centric week: Terra Moons x Ellie Starr “Starr Gazing” (palette, singles, glosses) and Blend Bunny x Jordan Liberty Masterclass V2 (structure palettes, liners, lip glazes) anchored feeds; supporting waves from Dualism holo liquids, Glaminatrix toppers, and alt-friendly drugstore lips.
- Engagement Temperature:
  - High enthusiasm and tagging; clear “I need this” energy on launch posts and roundup guides (blue-brown duos, holographics, mainstream iridescents).
  - Comments skewed purchase-minded (“picked up entire bundle,” “need all of them”), plus practical questions (sheerability, availability, brush size, setting spray behavior).

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Aurora Capsule: blue–green aurora palette + lime matte + iridescent/holo topper + sheer black gloss.
    - Primary: Terra Moons Starr Gazing (palette, singles “Zodiacal Light,” “Midnight Meridian,” glosses “Moonshadow,” “Alrescha”). If unavailable, sub with Clionadh (Grisaille/Abrasion), Rituel de Fille (Cygnus), Beauty Creations singles (Supernova), Wet n Wild Chameleon Chrome, Lethal “Opal,” Kaleidos Mooncruiser.
  - Alt Lip Bar (multi-budget):
    - Drugstore: Revlon (Midnight Mystery, Black Cherry, Va Va Violet), CoverGirl (Bestie Boo, Come Through).
    - Indie/Pro: Unearthly liners (Vamp/Char), KJH “Forge,” AORA black lip serum, Kaleidos Glassload Lip Melts; MAC Tilted Denim; L’Oréal Shine “Girls Night” (grey).
  - High-impact highlights: Unearthly Specter; Lethal x Dexter Luminol; Kaleidos highlighters (as eyeshadow toppers).
  - Precision tools: Curate smaller cheek/contour/blush brushes to meet “no-muddy” demand.

- Content Strategy:
  - High-performing formats to emulate:
    - Macro swatch carousels (blue–brown duos, holographics, iridescents) with daylight vs direct light.
    - 30–60s technique explainers (cheek zoning; foundation vs concealer order) paired with shoppable sets.
    - Lip combo shorts: sheer black gloss overlays; nude glaze + liner pairings; alt shades with liner tricks.
    - Multipurpose demos: face palettes on eyes; highlighters as inner-corner/lid sheen.
  - Community service:
    - “Where to buy” cards for hard-to-source items (J.Cat, Dualism prelaunch).
    - Shade maps for different skin tones (Blend Bunny structure palettes).

- Gap Analysis:
  - Demand for a purple in Dualism’s holo liquids—seek or request brand addition; stock an alt brand purple holo liquid as stopgap.
  - Accessible sheer black glosses: add dupes beyond Moonshadow to avoid OOS disappointment.
  - Availability friction on chrome liners: offer multichrome liners with broad stock; highlight eye-safety.
  - Tools gap: explicit “small brush kit” for blush/contour/highlight layering.

## 6. Special insights

- Emerging Micro-Trends:
  - Sheer black, high-gloss lips layered over deep liners (seen across Terra Moons, Amanda).
  - Grey/white mascara with icy/silver looks for editorial contrast.
  - Under-eye sparkle “tears” and blown-out glitter wings as intentional texture, not fallout.
  - “Pro neutral” face palettes used as matte eye scaffolding; magnetic, build-your-own travel quads.

- Unexpected Pairings:
  - Face highlights under-eye and on lips (Dieverune, Lethal Luminol).
  - Cream paint intended for eyes used on lips for cyber-metallic effects (not ideal texture—flag in copy).
  - Kryolan 151 pencil as a lip liner; KJH near-black contour pencil to blur lip edges.

- Creative insight:
  - “Spooky Spring” is crystallizing: pastel/cool eye stories (mint, lavender, baby blue) fused with goth signifiers (sheer black gloss, grey mascara, ghost embossing, chains). Bundle and market as a seasonal theme to bridge spring shoppers to alt aesthetics without abandoning depth or sparkle.
