# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Products Report

**Reporting Period:** February 02–08, 2026

1. **Dead Roses Vol II Palette by Bella Beauté Bar** — 17 mentions
   This palette dominated the week's conversation as a highly anticipated launch, with creators praising its "grungy" color story featuring muted olives, plums, and fading rose tones. Reviewers consistently highlighted the improved shimmer formula, describing it as "super sparkly" and non-flaky, while the mattes were lauded for their buildable, soft-touch pigmentation. It was frequently compared to the original Dead Roses palette, with creators noting it successfully captures the aesthetic of withered flora.

2. **Diabla Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 9 mentions
   Creators celebrated this collaboration palette for its intense pigmentation, particularly regarding the difficult-to-formulate red and deep pink shades. The "squishy" texture of the shimmers and the palette's ability to facilitate dramatic, alternative Valentine's Day looks were recurring themes. Influencers described it as a "masterpiece" and a "beaut," utilizing it for both graphic liner work and blown-out smoky eyes.

3. **Sparkle Lip Balms by Bella Beauté Bar** — 8 mentions
   Launched alongside the Dead Roses collection, these hybrid balm-lipsticks were praised for delivering high-impact sparkle without a gritty texture. Creators highlighted specific shades like "Shooting Star" and "Dead Roses" for their transformative, duochrome qualities that can be worn alone or as toppers. The formula was described as comfortable and hydrating, effectively bridging the gap between lip care and high-glam makeup.

4. **Made a Monster Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 7 mentions
   This palette was favored for its vibrant, almost fluorescent pinks and corals paired with deep, moody tones, making it a go-to for bold, high-contrast looks. Creators appreciated the variety of textures and the "attitude sassy" shade names, noting that the mattes blend seamlessly while the shimmers provide intense reflectivity. It was frequently cited as a key product for creating "menacing" or alternative aesthetic looks.

5. **Spectra Pens & Stamps by Glisten Cosmetics** — 7 mentions
   These graphic liner tools were heavily utilized by creators for adding intricate details, such as star stamps and precise lines, to complex eye looks. The products were praised for their opacity and versatility, allowing for creative expression beyond standard eyeshadow application. They appeared consistently in "get ready with me" content as essential tools for finishing editorial makeup styles.

6. **Succulent Dew Palette by Singe Beauty** — 6 mentions
   As a creator-owned brand release, this palette received high praise for its unique cool-toned color story featuring teals, purples, and berries. Reviewers highlighted the "incredible quality" of the shimmers and the thoughtful arrangement of the layout, describing it as a versatile staple that balances neutral utility with colorful depth.

7. **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 6 mentions
   This product appeared as a consistent staple in daily makeup routines, particularly for creators creating heavy, graphic eye looks. While often listed without extensive verbal commentary, its ubiquitous presence in product breakdowns signals it is a high-performance favorite for defining lashes against intense eyeshadow backdrops.

8. **Magnetic Mineralia Palette by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 5 mentions
   Creators championed this palette for its geology-inspired, cool-toned color story, with one influencer noting they had already "hit pan" on multiple shades. It was highlighted for its high-impact shimmers and unique "rock and gemstone" aesthetic, serving as a frequent comparison point for other grunge-leaning palettes released this week.

9. **Moonlight Muse Palette by Arttitude Cosmetics** — 5 mentions
   Described as a "spicy neutral" palette, this product was commended for its mix of wearable neutrals and smoky plum shades. Creators praised the "glamorous, ethereal" quality of the shimmers and the high pigment density of the mattes, noting that the multi-dimensional shifts added a sophisticated complexity to their looks.

10. **Angelic Palette by Cosmic Beauty** — 4 mentions
    This palette was recognized for its "whimsical" and "romantic" pastel color story, which creators found perfect for soft, ethereal Valentine's Day looks. Reviewers appreciated the brand's consistent formula quality, specifically noting the blendability of the mattes and the ability to pair the lighter shades with deeper palettes for contrast.
