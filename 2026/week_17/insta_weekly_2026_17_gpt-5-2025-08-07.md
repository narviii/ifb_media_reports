# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway (Apr 20–26, 2026): Cool‑toned grunge met pastel sparkle. Indie singles and collabs (Terra Moons, Bella Beauté Bar x Freya, Shine by SD x Deandra) drove looks, while lip content (blackened balms, frosted tints) and comparison/tutorial reels captured the most intent.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - Heavy on swatches and side‑by‑side comparisons (multichromes, highlighters, glitter toppers) with quick tutorial reels and GRWMs anchoring the week.
  - Launch cadence: BBB x Freya Yin & Yang (cool/gray neutrals + split cake liner), Shine by SD x Deandra singles (Rose Metal), What’s Up Beauty Skyfire, Half Magic x Euphoria reveal/PR looks, About‑Face highlighting pearls/palettes, Terra Moons Pastel Perihelion in looks and swatches.
- Engagement Temperature:
  - High excitement and tagging on collabs and giveaways; repeated “buying,” “when can I get it,” and code‑use comments on indie shadows and lip launches.
  - Frequent product Qs (wear/creasing, shade IDs, where to buy, region availability). Lip shade round‑ups and “cadaver lip” recs achieved breakout engagement.
