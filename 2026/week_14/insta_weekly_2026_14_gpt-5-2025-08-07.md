# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway: Mar 30–Apr 5, 2026 saw a decisive pivot to cool-toned “gloomy spring” aesthetics—gray lips, blue‑purple chromes, and murky greens—while tutorial-style reels and comparison swatches converted curiosity into clear buy signals across indie staples and K‑beauty crossovers.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - Tutorials and quick tips dominated (SPF over makeup, blush placement, fallout fixes, foundation matching).
  - Side‑by‑side swatch content surged—multi-brand chrome/highlighter and Super Shock showdowns drove saves and comments.
  - Aesthetic mood: goth/grunge meets spring via slate greys, teal/olive, and blue‑purple iridescents; “Gloomy Spring” overtook pastels.

- Engagement Temperature:
  - Hot. Threads were full of “need,” “adding to cart,” shade‑specific questions, and restock chatter. Highest resonance: lip layering demos (gray glosses), chrome swatch comps, and smoky eye GRWMs.

## 2. Product Momentum (Brand & Item Velocity)

- Top Mentioned Brands:
  - Glisten Cosmetics (Glis Gloss, pens/mascara), Danessa Myricks Beauty (Yummy Skin, Lightwork, minis), Blend Bunny x Jordan Liberty (Structure palette, Masterclass pencils), Melt Cosmetics (Twelfth Hour, Evergreens + gel liners), Dieverune (Silver and Cold; Thunderbolts), Terra Moons, Clionadh, ColourPop, Unearthly, Laneige, House of HUR, Jung Saem Mool.

- Viral Products:
  - Glisten Glis Gloss in Veil and Gray Area: multiple reels, layered looks, explicit purchase intent (“Think you’ve just convinced me to buy this gloss”).
  - Laneige cool-toned balms (Black Plum Glaze, Rose Jam, Strawberry Sprinkles, Lavender Frosting): strong novelty buzz (“the lip balm market is saturated…Laneige accomplished that”).
  - Blend Bunny x Jordan Liberty Structure palette + 917 pencil: praised blendability and true cool contour; comments confirm purchases via code.
  - Melt Evergreens + gel liners (Colibrí, Hellflower) and Twelfth Hour stack: repeated looks; comments “Now I have to get this.”
  - Blue‑purple chrome ecosystem: Terra Moons (Under the Violet Skies, Hyades Cluster), Clionadh (Regal, Enamel, Etched, Tessara), Karla Krazy Kat, ColourPop (Ripple/Cotton Candy), Moira Lilac Love; high swatch engagement.
  - Dieverune Silver and Cold (grey/teal) + Thunderbolts (moody shimmer): quality callouts; “favorite Dieverune palette to date.”
  - Fenty Beauty Violet Vices liquid blush and Lisa Eldridge Velatura Balms: high blendability + balmy skin trend.

- The “Look” Breakdown:
  - EYES: Blue‑purple chromes and moody teals/greens; duochrome/iridescent inner corners; taupe or deep green graphic wings; smoky but soft-blended lower lash saturation. Frequent toppers: Clionadh/Terra Moons/Sheeneffect.
  - LIPS: Gray/black balmy glosses layered over taupe/pale pink (Glisten Veil/Gray Area; Kaleidos Glass Load “Stranger” as gray mixer); vampy browns and plum stains (MAC Iconic Photo; Maybelline Speedwalk).
  - CHEEKS: Cream/liquid and balm textures (Fenty Violet Vices; Lisa Eldridge Velatura; Danessa minis). Finishes skew natural-dewy with colored highlighters (blue/green shifts) and strategic placement per new blush tutorials.

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Lip core build-out:
    - Glisten Glis Gloss (Veil, Gray Area) and a black gloss alternative; gray mixers (Kaleidos Glass Load “Stranger”).
    - Taupe/black lip-structure tools: Half Magic Sculptitude liner (Ur My Taupe/Badassery Parade), taupe liners (rom&nd Taupey; NYX Nude Truffle).
    - Vamp browns/plums (Maybelline Speedwalk; MAC deep browns). Consider J‑beauty “toner” balms à la KATE Lip Monster analogs if accessible.
  - Chrome/Topper wall:
    - Terra Moons (Under the Violet Skies; Hyades Cluster), Clionadh (Regal/Enamel/Etched/Tessara), Sheeneffect gradient shimmers; Dieverune Thunderbolts. Curate by particle size/texture (smooth foil vs sparkly).
  - Moody green/grey eye kits:
    - Melt Evergreens + gel liners; Dieverune Silver and Cold; Glisten Shape Shifter hi‑lite (yellow/green).
  - Skin prep and SPF that layer well:
    - Danessa Myricks minis (Prep & Prime Radiant; Blurring Balm), Jung Saem Mool Essential Mool + cushion foundation; House of HUR Weightless Sun Fluid; ONE/SIZE Until Dawn setting spray.

- Content Strategy:
  - Short, punchy reels:
    - Lip layering: gray gloss over pink/taupe; black-in-corners “moody up” demos.
    - “Blue‑purple chrome face‑off”: arm + eye swatches, fallout tests, primer vs. no primer.
    - “Gloomy Spring” GRWM: grey/teal palettes with duochrome inner corners.
    - Practical pro tips: SPF over makeup tap‑in method; blush placement sweet spot; fallout clean-up with last skincare step; waterline-safe black demo.
  - Comparison carousels outperform single-product posts in saves and buying Qs; cite prices/availability and texture notes.

- Gap Analysis:
  - Allergen-aware curation: multiple creators flagged tree‑nut oils (sweet almond) as widespread—curate nut‑free badges/filters and spotlight safe alternates.
  - Waterline‑safe black: with Blend Bunny’s gel out of stock, add reliable, ophthalmologist‑tested black gel pencil/pot and call out “waterline-safe.”
  - Cushion SPF gap: demand for “cushion sunscreen”—source weightless SPF cushions/mists that actually dose to PA/PPD targets; educate on quantity/application.

## 6. Special insights

- Emerging Micro-Trends:
  - Grey gloss revival crossing indie to mainstream (multiple creators, layered techniques, purchase intent).
  - Taupe wings/liners (Blend Bunny 917) replacing harsh black for graphic shapes.
  - Shimmery lip liners (Moira “Cupid Arrow”): metallic edge framing glossy centers.
  - “Gloomy Spring”: greys/teals as seasonal palette, not just winter.

- Unexpected Pairings:
  - Gel liners mixed into lipstick centers (Melt Colibrí/Hellflower into “Baked”).
  - Olive/green lip looks grounded with Kryolan green base; gray gloss over pale pink to desaturate without dulling shine.
  - Nail-art gems used as budget face gems (cost-saving hack with high payoff).

- Creative insight:
  - Bundle a “Gloomy Spring Kit”: taupe liner (917), moody green/yellow quad or stack, blue‑purple topper, gray gloss + taupe/black lip liner duo, plus a weightless SPF fluid and a grip setting spray. Market it as “cool‑tone confidence” with a 60‑second tutorial reel.
  - Build a “Chrome Bar” shopping filter: choose by shift (blue→purple, green→gold), particle size, and texture (smooth/foiled/sparkle), with a “least fallout” badge informed by creator tests.
