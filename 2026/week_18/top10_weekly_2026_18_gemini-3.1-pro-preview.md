# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** April 27–May 3, 2026

1. **Glitter Puck (Feel Something) by Half Magic** — 7 mentions  
   Creators are highly enthusiastic about this new release from the Euphoria collection, frequently using it as a multi-purpose highlighter for the face, eyes, and body. It is praised for its "ethereal sparkle," multidimensional blue/purple shifts, and ability to layer beautifully without a heavy base color.

2. **The Euphoria Eyeshadow Palette by Half Magic** — 7 mentions  
   Reviewers love the intense, ethereal duochrome shades (especially the blue-purple shift "Rue") and the approachable, smooth texture. The palette is frequently highlighted for its high-impact, sparkly finishes that perform beautifully as both eyeshadows and face highlighters.

3. **Greek Odyssey Eyeshadow Palette by Nomad Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   Creators are highly impressed by the unique cool-toned color story featuring blues, greens, and greys balanced with warm pops of shimmer. The matte formula is praised for being buttery and buildable, while the shimmers are celebrated for their multidimensional, "wet look" finishes.

4. **Spectra Lash Mascara by Glisten Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   This colorful mascara is celebrated for its impactful, bright pigmentation that shows up vibrantly even on dark lashes without needing a white base. Creators consistently note its impressive longevity, highlighting that it wears beautifully throughout the day without smudging or flaking.

5. **Barely Blushing Powder by Melt Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   This product is used consistently as a reliable setting powder across multiple high-glam and editorial makeup routines. Creators specifically highlight it as a beautiful, skin-like powder that sets the face flawlessly without looking dry or heavy.

6. **Ballet Beam Highlighting Pearls by about-face beauty** — 4 mentions  
   Creators are thrilled by the return of bold, blinding highlighters, praising the cream-like, lightweight texture of these pearls. The product is frequently used to deliver a seamless, high-shine glow to the cheekbones, nose, and cupid's bow, with users noting it builds up beautifully.

7. **Sparklestik by Half Magic** — 4 mentions  
   This product is frequently utilized as a quick, high-impact shimmer step in both everyday glam and alternative editorial routines. Creators rely on shades like "Champagne Snowflake" and "Noir or Never" to add a precise, reflective pop of sparkle to their eye looks.

8. **Stained Glass Multichrome Eyeshadows by Clionadh Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
   These shadows are heavily featured in dedicated swatch videos, with creators raving about their extraterrestrial glow and intense color shifts. The formula is highly regarded for its smooth texture and extreme, multicolored sparkle, particularly in the blue and purple shade ranges.

9. **Skyfire Palette by Whats Up Beauty** — 3 mentions  
   Reviewers appreciate the palette's travel-friendly magnetic format and its approachable color story that mixes vibrant hues with spicy neutrals. The matte formula is noted as uniquely thin and buildable, while the shimmers provide a smooth, highly sparkly finish without creasing.

10. **Liquid Bronzer by ColourPop** — 2 mentions  
    Creators are intrigued and impressed by this liquid formula, noting its excellent blendability and flattering satin finish. It is specifically praised for having a natural, neutral undertone that adds warmth without pulling too orange on the skin.
