# Weekly Instagram Beauty Media Landscape Report

- Key Takeaway: May 11–17, 2026 leaned cool, grunge, and sparkly: murky greens, icy silvers, and pink chromes dominated while high-utility tutorial Reels (lash application, blush/bronzer fixes) massively outperformed static posts. Indie shimmer launches and a Blend Bunny core restock fueled “add-to-cart” energy.

## 1. Overview

- The Vibe:
  - Tutorial-heavy week (lashes, blush/bronzer blending, rainbow eyes) alongside “nerd out” swatch reels for multichromes/pearlescents.
  - Event content (Half Magic “Club” night, Tower 28 plumper activation) added FOMO sparkle; fragrance sampling crossed into goth/alt beauty storytelling.
  - Visuals skewed cool-toned grunge with wet-look metallic lids, graphic liner touches, and unexpected lip shades (gunmetal, moss, vamp plums).

- Engagement Temperature:
  - Hot. Reels with step-by-step utility spiked: lash tutorial (271k plays, 23k likes), blush/bronzer hack (70k plays, 4.3k likes), rainbow mini-tutorial (102k plays, 9.4k likes).
  - Swatch explainers drove targeted buying questions (“Which shade?” “Dupes?” “White cast?”). Comments show explicit intent (“I need,” “Ordered,” “On my list”), restock asks, and shade recommendations.

## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Eyes:
    - Blend Bunny Blends 2.0 (core; with shimmers), x Mei Pang Rotten, Sickly Sweet; x Mei Pang E‑Fluorescence (iridescent toppers).
    - Dieverune Dewdrops 2 + singles (Silver Lining, Bismuth, Transmutation) and Rainbow HL.
    - Half Magic Glitter Puck (Feel Something, Princess Ghost, Cyber Diamond, Glimmer Pop, Dopamine Sparkle) + Euphoria palette.
    - Pink chromes: Terra Moons Redshift/Ring Nebula; Clionadh Pearlescents (Sunbeam, Renaissance); budget Wet n Wild chameleon chromes; Beauty Creations Supernova.
    - VIOLETTE_FR Plume (Poussière de Lune, Pluie Violette) or comparable putty singles.
    - What’s Up Beauty Skyfire for icy sparkles + deep mattes.
  - Lips/Cheeks:
    - Fenty Gloss Bomb Stix (deep vamp shades); Unearthly Vamp liner + Moon Water gloss; Kaleidos liners; green/grey/gunmetal lip options; Sushy Glow “dried blood” style blush; colored mascaras (red).

- Content Strategy:
  - Double down on practical Reel tutorials with clear steps and product callouts (lash fit/glue placement; blush/bronzer blend; one-and-done shimmers).
  - Side-by-side shade comparisons and “Which should I buy?” guides (Glitter Puck, pink chromes, vamp glosses) convert.
  - Macro eye/lip swatches in natural and flash lighting to showcase shift/texture; “Under $10 chromes vs indie” budget vs luxe content.
  - Event recaps/collabs (club nights/pop-ups) drive brand affinity—replicate locally.

- Gap Analysis:
  - Lash glue: consistent complaints about smelly/stringy formulas—stock brush-tip, low-odor, latex-free adhesives + mini how-to.
  - Tone-up confusion: curate non–tone-up bases; label warnings and education bite.
  - Access pain: Sheeneffect not shipping to some regions—offer domestic dupes/alternatives; collect waitlist interest.
  - Ethical tools: add premium synthetic “fude-style” brush sets as alternatives to natural hair.
  - EU availability: flag Half Magic access gaps; propose stocked alternatives with similar textures.

## 6. Special insights

  - Red mascara + red liquid liner with metallic red lids (monochrome eye).
  - Yellow/ochre blush placement across center of face (grunge sunburn effect).
  - Matte highlight revival for lift without shine (white/pearl powders on cheekbone/brow/Cupid’s bow).
  - Grey/gunmetal and moss/olive lip shades, often glossy or ombré with blue-violet shift.
