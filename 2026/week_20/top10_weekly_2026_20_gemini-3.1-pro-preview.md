# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** May 11–17, 2026

1. **Levitation Lash by Rituel de Fille** — 6 mentions  
   Used heavily by creator s.andromedae across multiple editorial and grunge-inspired looks. The mascara provides dark, defined, and voluminous lashes that perfectly complement heavy, blown-out eyeshadow styles. It is consistently applied alongside other Rituel de Fille complexion products to complete high-contrast, multi-chromatic aesthetics.

2. **Taupe Speckled Pen by Glisten Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
   Featured repeatedly by creator s.andromedae to add fine-line speckling and faux freckles to various makeup looks. The pen is used to create a natural, organic texture across the nose and cheeks that contrasts beautifully with heavy eye makeup. It seamlessly integrates into both colorful, galactic aesthetics and earthy, grunge-inspired styles.

3. **Moonlit Creature of Light by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   Frequently mixed with foundation or used as a primer by s.andromedae to create a luminous, soft-focus base. Creators rely on it to provide a natural, hydrated appearance that balances out heavy, matte editorial eye makeup. It serves as a reliable staple for achieving a glowing, dewy complexion.

4. **Intuition by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
   Used consistently as a contour product to add subtle, cool-toned sculpting to the cheekbones and jawline. The product provides a soft, diffused shadow that enhances facial structure without overpowering bold eye and lip choices. It is a go-to for achieving a perfectly balanced, softly sculpted complexion.

5. **Blends Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
   Highly praised for its recent restock and "2.0" upgrade, which features sleek new magnetic packaging and the addition of a row of shimmers. Creators like deandranicoleee highlighted its versatility, showcasing both intense, high-pigment rainbow looks and softer pastel applications. It is celebrated as an essential, core palette with a reliable, buttery matte formula and stunning new shimmer additions.

6. **Ellis Atlantis Vol 1 Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   Showcased by s.andromedae as a highly pigmented option for vibrant, color-blocked looks. Creators specifically highlighted its opaque matte shades, like forest green, and reflective, wet-look silver shimmers. It is favored for its high color payoff and ability to create sharp, graphic editorial styles.

7. **Sickly Sweet Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
   Used by creators like linsmakeuplooks and deandranicoleee to create vibrant, rainbow-themed eye looks. It is praised for its bright, dopamine-inducing color story that blends easily into intricate, multi-colored gradients. The palette is a favorite for packing intense, colorful pigments into the crease and inner corners.

8. **Skyfire Palette by Whats Up Beauty** — 3 mentions  
   Described by creators as having an aurora-inspired color story with smooth, pigmented mattes and show-stopping shimmers. The multichrome and metallic shades are praised for their icy, light-reflecting glow that mimics the Northern Lights. It is highly recommended for users who want intense, sparkly finishes paired with deep, smoky mattes.

9. **Shimmer Stay Primer by Bella Beauté Bar** — 3 mentions  
   Consistently used by YouTube creator battybean as a reliable base to ensure multichrome and shimmer shadows pop to their fullest potential. It is considered an essential, non-negotiable step for locking indie shimmers in place. The primer prevents creasing and maximizes the high-impact reflection of complex eyeshadow formulas.

10. **Plume Eyeshadows by Violette_fr** — 3 mentions  
    Praised for their putty-like, dimethicone-forward texture that applies effortlessly with a finger or brush. The formula blends out easily and lays flush on the skin, making it ideal for smudgy, undone, or "moon dust" textured eye looks. Creators love the chic, translucent blue compacts and the mix of earthy neutrals with metallic finishes.
