# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** November 03–09, 2025

1.  **Gingerbread Bakery Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 10 mentions  
    Creators consistently praised this palette for its festive theme, highlighting the adorable packaging and versatile color story of pastels and baked-good tones. The formula received positive feedback across the board for being highly pigmented, smooth, and easy to work with for both dramatic and sparkly holiday looks. Several creators also noted the thoughtful layout, which is designed to help users create cohesive looks.

2.  **Christkindlmarkt Palette by Nomad Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
    This palette was celebrated for perfectly capturing a festive Christmas market theme, with a classic holiday color story of reds, greens, and blues. Creators praised Nomad's reliable formula, noting the mattes blend easily and the shimmers are highly dimensional and sparkly. The unique pan layout, resembling a Christmas tree, was also frequently mentioned as a creative and appealing design feature.

3.  **Blush Filter Blurring Blushlighters Palette by Huda Beauty** — 7 mentions  
    This palette was frequently discussed for its unique "blurring" formula that delivers a beautiful, non-flat matte finish with intense color vibrancy. Creators were particularly drawn to the bright pink shades, using them to create glowy, high-impact cheek looks. While some mentioned skipping the purchase due to having similar products, the overall sentiment was positive regarding its performance and aesthetic appeal.

4.  **Rotten Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
    The Rotten palette was a standout favorite, frequently praised for containing what one creator called their "favorite matte formula" for being exceptionally pigmented, blendable, and long-lasting. It was consistently used to create vibrant, deep, and grungy looks, demonstrating its versatility for alternative and creative makeup styles. The palette's ability to build contrast was a key highlight for creators.

5.  **Come Play With Us Palette by Bella Beauté Bar** — 5 mentions  
    This palette generated excitement for its unique theme, described as a blend of spooky and winter holiday aesthetics inspired by "The Shining." Creators highlighted the excellent quality of the special shades, praising the intense pigmentation, variety, and complex, dimensional shifts. The visual appeal and high performance of the shimmers were consistently noted as a key strength.

6.  **Spectra Lash by Glisten Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    The Spectra Lash colored mascaras were a staple component in several creative and alternative makeup looks this week. Creators utilized various shades, including white, black, and soybean, to add a final graphic or colorful touch to their eye makeup. Its consistent use across multiple distinct looks suggests it is a go-to product for artists seeking to add precise, colorful details.

7.  **Speckled Pens by Glisten Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    These pens were repeatedly used by creators to apply realistic-looking faux freckles, adding detail and texture to their finished makeup. The taupe and russet shades were specifically mentioned for creating a natural effect. The product appears to be a reliable tool for achieving this popular aesthetic detail in a variety of looks.

8.  **Soft Pinch Liquid Blush by Rare Beauty** — 3 mentions  
    Creators expressed strong favoritism for this liquid blush, praising its sheer, buildable formula that provides a long-lasting, natural skin finish. It was frequently described as a "favorite" and a "so good" product, with creators choosing to keep multiple shades in their personal collections. The positive sentiment was consistent across all mentions.

9.  **Highlighters (Fanged Flora/Ghost Orchid) by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    These highlighters received enthusiastic praise for their exceptionally sparkly, glossy, and high-shine finish with complex, cool-toned shifts. Creators described them as a standout product from the brand, with one noting they are consistently "astounded" by the quality. The visual impact and intense, multi-dimensional reflect were the primary reasons for the positive sentiment.

10. **Nevermore Palette by Gourmande Girls** — 3 mentions  
    The Nevermore palette was highlighted for its unique grungy and colorful theme, with creators loving its purple and green color story. The matte formula was praised for its pigmentation, while the shimmers were noted for their glossy, sparkly finish. Despite one mixed review regarding a specific shade choice, the overall sentiment leaned positive, with one creator calling it their favorite palette from the brand.
