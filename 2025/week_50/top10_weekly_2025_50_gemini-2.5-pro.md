# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** December 8–14, 2025

1.  **Aurora Chasing Palette by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 10 mentions  
    This collaboration palette with creator Ellie Starr dominated social feeds ahead of its December 12th launch. Creators expressed strong positive sentiment, praising its aurora-themed color story, packaging, and high-performance formulas. Mentions consisted of unboxings, detailed swatches showcasing the pigmented mattes and sparkly shimmers, and full makeup tutorials.

2.  **Super Shock Cheek/Highlighter Formula by ColourPop** — 10 mentions  
    The Super Shock formula was a recurring favorite across creator declutters and tutorials. Creators consistently praised its unique, soft texture for both highlighters and blushes, often keeping products for their nostalgic packaging and reliable performance. The formula was highlighted for providing a beautiful glow without stickiness, with specific mentions of the Flexitarian shade and various limited edition collections.

3.  **Low Light Highlighter by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
    This product line was featured heavily in a major declutter video where a creator declared it her favorite highlighter formula. She kept a wide array of shades, from peachy-pinks to blood red and green, praising them for being iridescent, gorgeous, and fun. The sentiment was overwhelmingly positive, solidifying the product's status as a collection favorite.

4.  **Merry Chrissy Palette by Glaminatrix Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
    This Australian Christmas-themed palette was praised for its cute artwork and unique color story combining neutrals with festive pops of color. Creators showcased the palette through enthusiastic unboxings, detailed swatches, and full tutorials demonstrating its vibrant matte and glitter performance. The overall sentiment was highly positive, with creators enjoying the palette's "whole package" appeal for holiday looks.

5.  **Haunt Me Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
    This new palette generated significant buzz, with creators praising its well-balanced warm and cool color story and high-quality, blendable formulas. It was highlighted for its magnetic pans and good value, with mentions often tied to the launch of a coordinating lip collection. Creators used the palette for tutorials, expressing excitement and strong positive sentiment.

6.  **TerraStarr Highlighter by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
    Launched alongside the Aurora Chasing palette, this collaboration highlighter was lauded for its intensely sparkly, wet-look finish. Creators were impressed by its transparent base that delivered a blinding shine without leaving a cast, noting its smooth texture. It was frequently used on the high points of the face and as a high-impact inner corner highlight.

7.  **Ombre Chrome Shadows by Sheeneffect** — 5 mentions  
    This new collection of single shadows from the C-beauty brand was heavily featured by a creator specializing in sparkly makeup. The products were praised for their unique ombre-in-pan design and intoxicating, multi-chrome shifts. Mentions included detailed swatches of the full collection and a tutorial demonstrating how the shadows apply with intense, multi-colored sparkle.

8.  **Highlighter by Lethal Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    Featured in a declutter video, the brand's highlighters were consistently kept and praised for their performance and beauty. Creators described the various formulas as stunning, offering effects from a wet-look blue iridescent glow to a unique rainbow shimmer. The sentiment was very positive regarding the product itself, even with minor critiques of packaging.

9.  **Soft Pinch Liquid Blush by Rare Beauty** — 3 mentions  
    This viral liquid blush was featured in tutorials demonstrating its powerful pigmentation. Creators used it as a vibrant base for layered blush looks and also provided educational content on how to control the intense formula. The discussion focused on its performance and blendability, reinforcing its reputation as a highly pigmented, impactful product.

10. **Mostly Merry Palette by Gourmande Girls** — 3 mentions  
    This festive palette was highlighted for its unique and charming snow globe packaging. Creators enthusiastically unboxed and swatched the shades, showcasing its rich mattes and highly pigmented glitters. It was used to create simple yet effective holiday-themed eye looks, with positive sentiment centered on its theme and performance.
