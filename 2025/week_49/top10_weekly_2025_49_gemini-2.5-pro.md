# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** December 01–07, 2025

1.  **Crystallize Palette by Bella Beaute Bar** — 21 mentions  
    Creators overwhelmingly praised this palette for its dreamy, cool-toned winter color story, viewing it as a refreshing alternative to traditional holiday themes. It was frequently described as a "stunner" with creamy, pigmented mattes and standout ethereal, shifty multi-chrome shimmers. Discussions ranged from detailed swatch reviews and tutorials to creators simply expressing their love for its unique aesthetic, solidifying its position as a new favorite for many.

2.  **Dead Cute Lipsticks by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 12 mentions  
    This lipstick collection was celebrated for its unique and unconventional "funky" shades, including a standout light grey ("Ghost Milk") and shifty metallics. Creators praised the creamy, long-wearing formula and high pigmentation, often showcasing the products in lip swatch videos and tutorials. The grey lipstick was frequently highlighted as a versatile mixing shade for creating custom cool-toned lip combinations, generating significant positive buzz.

3.  **Mostly Merry Palette by Gourmande Girls Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
    This palette garnered attention primarily for its unique, collectible snow-globe packaging and cool-toned winter color story. Creators described the theme as a "snowy landscape," praising the brand's buttery matte formula and intensely sparkly, reflective shimmers. While the aesthetic and shade selection were well-received, some creators noted practical considerations like the palette's shape for storage.

4.  **Nocturnal Flame Palette by Shellwe Makeup x Ghoul.in.japan** — 7 mentions  
    This collaboration palette was highly praised for its unique and unusual color story, featuring a mix of cool-toned neutrals with striking pops of mint blue and orange. Creators consistently highlighted its grungy, goth-inspired aesthetic, noting that it perfectly captured the collaborator's personal style. The palette was featured in numerous tutorials where creators expressed strong positive sentiment and loved the creative possibilities it offered.

5.  **Cosmic Shift Palette by Kristen Leigh Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
    This all-special-shade palette generated significant excitement for its intense sparkle and dramatic color shifts, featuring a mix of multichromes, holochromes, and foiled formulas. Creators described it as a "jaw-dropper" for shimmer enthusiasts, highlighting the impactful, multidimensional sparkle in detailed swatch videos. The palette's high quality and unique composition led to it selling out quickly, with discussions immediately turning to a potential pre-order.

6.  **Gingerbread Bakery Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
    This palette received attention for its cute, whimsical gingerbread theme and a color story blending pastels with warm neutrals. Creators generally praised the beautiful, wet-looking shimmers, though reviews on the matte formula were mixed, with some finding the pastels needed building up or were too thin. Overall sentiment was positive about the aesthetic, but its specific color combination made it a personal preference item.

7.  **Artificer Palette by Fantasy Cosmetica** — 5 mentions  
    This palette was positively received for its "spicy neutral steampunk-y" theme and high-quality formula. Creators consistently praised the brand's incredibly pigmented mattes and gorgeous, shifty shimmers in tutorials and reviews. While the overall quality was lauded, some discussion centered on the specific neutral color story and whether it provided enough contrast for individual preferences.

8.  **Dead Cute Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
    Creators celebrated this palette as a fun, loud, and playful offering with an "acid pastel rainbow" color story. It was frequently featured in tutorials and swatch videos, where its glossy shimmers and easily blendable mattes were praised. The overall sentiment was highly positive, with creators enjoying the vibrant and cutesy-spooky aesthetic, despite one mention of some shades feeling slightly hard-pressed.

9.  **Ice Crystal Palette by Oden's Eye** — 5 mentions  
    This palette was discussed for its whimsical wintery theme and high-quality pastel shades, with creators particularly praising the shiny, wet-looking shimmers. However, reviews were somewhat mixed, as its large physical size was a drawback for some creators. The color story was also critiqued for being too light-leaning, lacking the deeper matte shades needed for versatile looks.

10. **Serpentine Palette by Lethal Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    Creators praised this palette for its unique and dark color story, which creatively combined muted tones with pops of purple and green. The snake-themed packaging was described as gorgeous, and the formula was well-regarded for its pigmented, blendable mattes and "incredibly beautiful" multichrome shades. The palette was highlighted as a strong non-collaboration release with a creative and cohesive theme.
