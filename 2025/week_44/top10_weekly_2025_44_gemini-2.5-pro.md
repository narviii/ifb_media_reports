# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** October 27–November 02, 2025

1.  **Christkindlmarkt Palette by Nomad Cosmetics** — 5 mentions  
    Creators celebrated this palette for its festive theme, nostalgic packaging, and high-performance shadows perfect for holiday looks. It was praised for its blendable mattes and highly pigmented, multi-dimensional shimmers that were showcased in multiple tutorials. The brand's decision to make it a permanent item was also noted as a positive.

2.  **Lightwork VII The Freedom Makeup Palette by Danessa Myricks Beauty** — 5 mentions  
    Hailed as a standout release, this palette was lauded for its ingenious magnetic and customizable design. Creators expressed significant excitement over the inclusion of matte shades alongside unique multichromes and shimmers, calling the textures beautiful and innovative. The palette was strongly recommended as a must-have, with creators showcasing its versatility in unboxings and tutorials.

3.  **The Witch's Familiar Eyeshadow Palette by Fantasy Cosmetica** — 4 mentions  
    This collaboration palette was highlighted for its strong performance and magical aesthetic. Creators used its smooth purple mattes and intense green duochrome shimmers to build complex, high-impact eye looks. The palette's quality and color story were further showcased in dedicated swatch videos, indicating strong positive sentiment.

4.  **Pacific Northwest Palette by Nomad Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    This palette was praised for its unique, desaturated, and grungy color story that creators found beautiful and thematically appropriate. The brand was commended for its consistent quality improvement, with the shadows described as highly pigmented and blendable. Its unique aesthetic was also noted as a perfect match for looks inspired by the Pacific Northwest setting of the Twilight saga.

5.  **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 4 mentions  
    This mascara appeared as a clear staple product for one creator, who featured it in multiple distinct makeup looks throughout the week. Its consistent use to complete various eye looks suggests it is a reliable and favored mascara for performance. The creator's affiliate code for the brand further implies a strong endorsement.

6.  **Come Play With Us Palette by Bella Beaute Bar** — 3 mentions  
    Inspired by The Shining, this palette was celebrated for its unique theme, unusual color story, and high-quality performance. Creators praised its vibrant reds and impactful shimmers, with one showcasing its blendable mattes in a detailed graphic liner tutorial. The palette was described as a standout product that was quickly becoming a favorite from the brand.

7.  **The Uniform Tinted Mineral Sunscreen by Merit** — 3 mentions  
    This tinted sunscreen received praise for its genuinely sheer, lightweight formula that provides high SPF protection. Creators highlighted its hydrating yet non-greasy feel, making it a comfortable favorite for daily wear. Its reliable performance also made it a recommended product for travel.

8.  **x Dexter Collection by Lethal Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This collaboration was featured across multiple product categories, indicating a positive reception. The eyeshadow palette and lip stain were used to create cohesive looks, while the nail polishes were praised for their excellent formula and ease of application. The collection's thematic elements were used to create distinct, coordinated beauty looks.

9.  **Ghost Orchid Highlighter by Dieverune Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This highlighter was consistently featured for its unique, ethereal blue shift. Creators used it to add a striking glow to the high points of the face and the cupid's bow. Its inclusion in a "best of 2025" list for blue highlighters confirms its status as a standout product for achieving an unconventional, iridescent finish.

10. **Bouclé Silk Airbrushed Finishing Powder by ONE/SIZE** — 2 mentions  
    This product was highly praised by creators as a "go-to" and "favorite" finishing powder for achieving a healthy, skin-like glow. It was consistently described as providing a silky, airbrushed finish without any glitter. Creators recommended it as a final step to meld makeup together and restore a natural luminosity to the skin.
