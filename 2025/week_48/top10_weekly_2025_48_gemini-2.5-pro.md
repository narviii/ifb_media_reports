# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** November 24–29, 2025

1.  **Pressed Single Shadow by Lethal Cosmetics** — 54 mentions  
    This product line dominated discussion due to a creator reorganizing their extensive collection, providing overwhelmingly positive feedback. Individual shades were repeatedly described as "so pretty," "beautiful," and "so good," with creators praising their unique multichrome shifts, holographic effects, and vibrant metallic finishes. The sheer volume of mentions highlights a deep appreciation for the brand's extensive and high-quality single shadow range.

2.  **Lip Melts by Kaleidos** — 19 mentions  
    Creators consistently described this product as a "comfy lip balm/gloss hybrid" with a creamy, shiny, and non-sticky texture. Mentions highlighted the diverse shade range, from a cool gray ("Stranger") to sparkly glitters ("Cypher") and warm browns ("End Game"), often used to complete specific themed looks. Overall sentiment was highly positive, emphasizing the product's comfortable wear and visually appealing finishes.

3.  **Death Tarot Palette by Cosmic Beauty** — 12 mentions  
    This new launch generated significant excitement, with creators praising its unique gothic, cool-toned color story and unexpected pops of color. The palette was described as versatile and inspiring, with mentions highlighting its blendable mattes, special shimmers, and the brand's consistent quality. The "Death" tarot theme was a key point of discussion, with creators appreciating its interpretation as renewal and rebirth.

4.  **Festive Elegance Palette by Dede's Signature** — 12 mentions  
    Creators universally lauded this palette for perfectly embodying a holiday theme with its rich, jewel-toned color story of greens, reds, and golds. The formula was praised for its high pigmentation, blendable mattes, and "stunning" sparkly shimmers. The festive packaging and ability to create luxurious, "in your face Christmas" looks were consistently highlighted as major strengths.

5.  **Lip Liners by Kaleidos** — 12 mentions  
    The new lip liners from Kaleidos received positive feedback for their creamy texture and good pigmentation, making them easy to work with. Creators showcased a wide range of nude and deep shades, such as "Double Espresso" and "Dark Matter," using them to define lips and create a base for lip combos. The overall sentiment pointed to a successful launch of a versatile and high-performing new product line.

6.  **Moonlit Metal Topper by Menagerie Cosmetics** — 12 mentions  
    This product was featured in a creator's collection review, where most shades were praised for their continued high quality, sparkle, and "gorgeous" appearance. However, the review also noted a significant downside, with four shades having dried out and become unusable. This indicates a mixed experience of beautiful performance but potential longevity issues.

7.  **Epiphany Glow Melt-on Eyeliner by Kaleidos** — 11 mentions  
    This product line, featuring new shade extensions, was celebrated for its vibrant, shifty colors and long-wearing, budge-proof formula. Creators used shades like the "pretty shifty blue" Miss Matrix and the "stunning" red Scarlet Striker to create bold, graphic liner looks. The eyeliners were consistently praised for their performance and unique, eye-catching shades.

8.  **Infinity Lip Stain by Lethal Cosmetics** — 10 mentions  
    This new launch was met with excitement, particularly for its luxe packaging and unique shade range inspired by "poisonous flowers." Creators praised the non-drying, comfortable, and long-lasting formula, noting its versatility for creating both sheer and bold looks on lips and cheeks. The easy-to-control applicator and lasting power were key positive points.

9.  **Serpentine Palette by Lethal Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
    The new Serpentine Palette was highly anticipated and praised for its "gorgeous" snake-themed packaging and inspiring color story. Creators were excited by the mix of neutrals and colors, particularly the combination of raspberry pink and neon green, and the variety of finishes including duo-chromes and multi-chromes. The palette was seen as a versatile and high-quality release from the newly rebranded brand.

10. **Evocative Silk Blush by SINGE Beauty** — 6 mentions  
    These blushes were consistently praised by creators, including the brand owner, for their "satin glow" finish that is glowy without being glittery. The formula was described as buildable, pigmented, and easy to work with. Mentions highlighted specific shades like "Rivetings Rhubarb" and noted the product's effectiveness in creating a beautiful, silky finish on the cheeks.
