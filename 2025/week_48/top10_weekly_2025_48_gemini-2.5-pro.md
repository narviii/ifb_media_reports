# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** November 24–29, 2025

1.  **Pressed Single Shadow by Lethal Cosmetics** — 54 mentions  
    This product's high mention count is driven by a single creator's comprehensive swatch video of their extensive collection. The creator expressed overwhelmingly positive sentiment, repeatedly praising individual shades for their unique multichrome shifts, vibrant pigmentation, and beautiful finishes. Recurring descriptions included "so pretty," "gorgeous," and "lovely," highlighting strong satisfaction with the formula's quality and aesthetic appeal.

2.  **Lip Pencils by Kaleidos** — 23 mentions  
    Creators consistently praised this product line for its high-performance formula, describing it as exceptionally "creamy" and "smooth & blendable" while also being long-lasting. The pencils were frequently used to line and fill the lips, serving as a versatile base for various lip combinations. The sentiment was uniformly positive, with a focus on the comfortable, easy-to-work-with texture and the well-curated range of shades.

3.  **Lip Melts by Kaleidos** — 19 mentions  
    This product was widely praised as an innovative "lip balm/gloss hybrid," celebrated for its comfortable, non-gritty formula and high-shine finish. Creators frequently highlighted the sparkly shades, describing them as "ultra sparkly and special" and visually appealing. The Lip Melts were used to complete a variety of looks, both on their own for a sheer wash of color and layered over lip liners for a more defined, glossy effect.

4.  **Festive Elegance Palette by Dede Signature** — 14 mentions  
    This newly launched holiday palette generated significant positive buzz, with creators describing it as "gorgeous," "luxurious," and a perfect embodiment of the festive season. The color story, featuring rich jewel-toned reds, greens, and golds, was consistently praised for its vibrancy and unique take on classic Christmas colors. Creators highlighted the high quality of the pigmented mattes and "special" sparkly shimmers, with many calling it one of their favorite holiday palettes.

5.  **Moonlit Metal Topper by Menagerie Cosmetics** — 13 mentions  
    This product's mentions stem from a creator reviewing their personal collection, resulting in mixed feedback. While many shades were still described as "gorgeous" and "so sparkly," confirming their continued aesthetic appeal, a notable number of the liquid eyeshadows had dried out and were no longer usable. This indicates strong initial performance and beauty but raises potential concerns about the product's long-term shelf life.

6.  **Death Tarot Palette by Cosmic Beauty** — 12 mentions  
    This new palette was met with widespread excitement, largely due to its unique tarot theme and "moody" yet versatile color story. Creators praised the interesting combination of cool tones, grungy shades, and unexpected brights, noting the formula was pigmented and easy to blend. The palette was consistently described as "so pretty," with its special shimmers and cohesive aesthetic generating highly positive first impressions and reviews.

7.  **Epiphany Glow Eyeliners by Kaleidos** — 12 mentions  
    Creators celebrated this eyeliner for its exceptional performance, highlighting its "creamy, glidy" application and "budge proof" longevity. The new multi-chrome shades received particular attention, with creators showcasing their unique, shifty colors in swatches and graphic liner looks. The product was consistently recommended for its vibrant pigmentation and reliable, long-lasting formula.

8.  **Serpentine Palette by Lethal Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
    This new palette was a highlight of the brand's Black Friday launch, earning praise for its "gorgeous" snake-themed packaging and inspiring color story. Creators were excited by the mix of versatile neutrals and unique pops of color, including a neon green and various multi-chrome finishes. The palette was presented as a high-quality, desirable item with a versatile formula suitable for creating a range of looks.

9.  **Brushes by Rephr** — 7 mentions  
    These brushes were consistently recommended by a creator who considers them personal favorites, highlighting their high quality and performance. Specific brushes were praised for their effectiveness in both face and eye makeup application, with the overall collection described as "beautiful" and "handcrafted." The mentions suggest these are considered reliable, high-performing tools for achieving a polished makeup look.

10. **Infinity Lip Stains by Lethal Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
    This new lip stain collection was positively received for its beautiful packaging and unique, non-drying formula. Creators were excited by the shade range, inspired by poisonous flowers, and demonstrated its versatility for creating both bold and sheer lip looks. The product was praised for being comfortable and easy to apply, making it a standout from the brand's new launch.
