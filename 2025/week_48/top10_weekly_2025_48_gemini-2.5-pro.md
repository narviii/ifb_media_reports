# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** November 24–29, 2025

1.  **Pressed Single Shadow by Lethal Cosmetics** — 53 mentions  
    The product's dominance was driven by a single creator's comprehensive review of her personal collection, where she swatched and described dozens of shades. The sentiment was overwhelmingly positive, with consistent praise for the vibrant pigmentation, unique multichrome and holographic shifts, and overall high quality of the formula.

2.  **Lip Melts by Kaleidos** — 16 mentions  
    As part of a new collection, creators consistently praised this product for its comfortable "lip balm/gloss hybrid" formula. It was frequently described as creamy, shiny, and easy to apply, with various shades used to complete creative makeup looks. The sentiment was uniformly positive, focusing on the product's comfortable texture and appealing finish.

3.  **Festive Elegance Palette by Dede's Signature** — 13 mentions  
    This palette received widespread positive attention for its festive, holiday-inspired color story. Creators described it as "gorgeous" and "luxurious," praising the rich pigmentation of the jewel-toned mattes and the high-impact sparkle of the shimmers. The palette was consistently lauded for its beautiful packaging and ability to create elegant holiday looks.

4.  **Moonlit Metal Topper by Menagerie Cosmetics** — 13 mentions  
    This product was featured in a creator's collection review, generating mixed but mostly positive feedback. Many shades were praised for being "gorgeous" and "so sparkly," demonstrating continued high quality and vibrant finishes. However, several older products were noted as having dried out, indicating some concerns about the formula's long-term stability.

5.  **Death Palette by Cosmic Beauty** — 11 mentions  
    This new palette generated significant excitement across platforms for its unique tarot theme and moody, cool-toned color story. Creators expressed high praise for its versatile mix of muted and bright shades, pigmented mattes, and special, sparkly shimmers. The overall sentiment was extremely positive, with many thrilled by the brand's creative direction and formula quality.

6.  **Lip Liners by Kaleidos** — 11 mentions  
    Launched as part of a new collection, these lip liners were well-received for their creamy texture and good pigmentation. Creators demonstrated their versatility by using various nude and dark shades to precisely define lips and as a base for different lip combinations. The sentiment was positive, focusing on the formula's blendability and ease of use.

7.  **Serpentine Palette by Lethal Cosmetics** — 6 mentions  
    This new palette was a highlight of the brand's Black Friday launch, earning praise for its "stunning" snake-themed packaging and inspiring color story. Creators were particularly excited by the mix of mattes, metallics, and multichromes, using the vibrant greens and pinks to create impactful looks. The overall sentiment was highly positive, driven by both the aesthetic and the quality of the eyeshadows.

8.  **Snowdrop Color Nectar Pigment Balm by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
    This multi-use pigment balm was featured as part of the brand's Black Friday collection, often as a gift with purchase. Creators showcased its versatility by applying the "cool cherry blossom pink" shade on eyes, cheeks, and lips. The product was positively received for its vibrant color and multi-functional use.

9.  **Apparition Rare Light Crème Luminizer by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
    This cream luminizer was praised for delivering a "fuchsia flash" of color with a pure, sparkle-free glow. Creators loved how the formula provided a radiant, "from-within" effect when applied to the cheekbones. As part of a new holiday collection, it was consistently highlighted for its unique finish and vibrant shade.

10. **Midnight Glow Thorn Bite Peptide Plump Crème Lip Oil by Rituel de Fille** — 5 mentions  
    This lip oil was a standout from the brand's new collection, noted for its unique color and high-shine, sparkly finish. Creators described it as a comfortable, hydrating formula with a beautiful rust-red base infused with green and blue sparkle. The sentiment was positive, focusing on its comfortable wear and special, multi-dimensional appearance.
