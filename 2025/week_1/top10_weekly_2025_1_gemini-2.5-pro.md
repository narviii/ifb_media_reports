# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** January 01–05, 2025

1. **Mini 6 Pan Palettes by Glaminatrix Cosmetics** — 1 mention  
   This collection was reviewed by a creator who received it in PR, noting the curated color stories and travel-friendly magnetic format. The brand's sparkly eyeshadow formula was praised as impactful and high-quality, while the mattes were described as pigmented and blendable. A minor critique was that the topper shades across the different palettes were somewhat similar.

2. **Entrancing Eyes Palette by Glaminatrix Cosmetics** — 1 mention  
   Featured within a review of the brand's mini palette collection, this specific pink-toned palette was highlighted for its unique qualities. Its topper shade was described as the most distinct in the collection, having a light shift and a pink lean. The creator expressed a strong positive sentiment, stating they "really love" this particular palette.

3. **Exquisite Eyes Palette by Glaminatrix Cosmetics** — 1 mention  
   This palette was singled out for having the creator's favorite topper shade, which was described as feeling more "piecey and sparkly" than others in its collection. The matte shades also received specific praise for offering a good range of tone and depth. The overall context was a positive review of the brand's new releases.
