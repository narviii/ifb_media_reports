# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** December 15–21, 2025

1.  **Holiday Palettes (Re-release) by Oden's Eye** — 13 mentions  
    This re-release, offered as singles with an updated formula, was a major topic of discussion, with creators calling it a clever business move. The palettes were praised for their luxurious, smooth mattes and more sparkly, high-performance shimmers. Creators used both the warm-toned (Merry Christmas) and cool-toned (Christmas Eve) color stories for festive tutorials and swatch-focused content, highlighting their intense pigmentation.

2.  **Masterclass Pro Pencils by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 11 mentions  
    These multi-use pencils, especially the deep red 'Bloodline' and black 'Rated R' shades, were frequently used for dramatic lip and eye looks. Creators consistently praised the creamy, waterproof formula for its intense pigmentation and ease of use on the waterline and for sharp lip definition. The launch was met with excitement, with creators already requesting an expansion of the shade range.

3.  **Haunt Me Palette by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
    Described overwhelmingly as a "dark, grungy dream," this palette was celebrated for its cool-toned, spooky aesthetic and high-quality formulas. Creators utilized its blendable mattes and special effect shimmers to create complex, gothy, and smoky eye looks. The palette's thematic organization into quads also received positive mentions for inspiring cohesive, alternative styles.

4.  **Masterclass Lip Oil/Gloss by Blend Bunny Cosmetics** — 7 mentions  
    This formula was praised for delivering a highly glossy, pigmented, and wet-look finish. Creators highlighted the unique sparkly shades like the sheer black 'Premonition' and the multi-sparkle 'Ghosted' for creating dimensional effects. The product was frequently shown in unboxings and used in lip combinations, with positive remarks on its luxurious feel and appearance.

5.  **Lip Melt by Kaleidos** — 5 mentions  
    Creators described this newer lip formula as exceptionally comfortable, cool, and high-shine. It was consistently used as a finishing touch to provide a dimensional, "melted" effect to lip looks. Specific shades were chosen to complete makeup looks, and creators positively noted the product's cooling sensation and glossy impact.

6.  **Crystallize Palette by Bella Beaute Bar** — 4 mentions  
    This palette was commended for its unique "dingy dusty yummy winter" aesthetic and soft, muted color story. Creators loved its beautiful, shifty shimmers and used its cool-toned blues, purples, and greens to create moody looks. The overall sentiment was very positive, with creators appreciating its unique take on a crystal theme beyond typical jewel tones.

7.  **Eyeliner Pencils by Kaleidos** — 4 mentions  
    These eyeliners were a functional favorite, used to intensify colorful eyeshadow looks with a precise pop of color. Creators selected specific shades like the dark blue 'Miss Matrix' and red 'Scarlet Striker' to perfectly complement the shimmers in their palettes. The pencils were praised for their strong pigment payoff and effectiveness in defining the waterline and lower lash line.

8.  **Levitation Lash Mascara by Rituel de Fille** — 4 mentions  
    This mascara appeared consistently across multiple Instagram posts from a single creator to complete various dramatic and editorial eye looks. While not the central focus, its repeated use as a finishing step implies it is a reliable, go-to product for achieving desired lash effects. The context was purely functional, suggesting consistent and trusted performance.

9.  **Gothic Romance Palette by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This palette received high praise, with one creator calling it "one of the best eyeshadow palettes of the year" and a "dark horse." The associated blush palette from the collection was also used to complement looks, reinforcing the collection's positive reception. The product was consistently lauded for its beautiful quality and moody, romantic aesthetic.

10. **Merry Chrissy Palette by Glaminatrix** — 3 mentions  
    Creators celebrated this palette for its "uniquely specific" and fun Australian (summertime) Christmas theme. The color story was praised for its ability to capture classic holiday colors with a distinctively warmer twist. The palette's shimmers were a standout feature, highlighted by creators for their high impact in festive eye looks.
