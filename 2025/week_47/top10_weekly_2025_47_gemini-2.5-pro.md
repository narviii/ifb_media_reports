# Weekly Top 10 Indie Makeup Product Mentions

**Reporting Period:** November 17–23, 2025

1.  **Artificer Palette by Fantasy Cosmetica** — 17 mentions  
    This palette was the subject of numerous dedicated reviews and tutorials across both platforms, receiving overwhelmingly positive feedback. Creators described its theme as "steampunky spicy neutrals," praising the high pigmentation of its versatile mattes and the unique, complex formulas of its shimmers and multichromes. The palette was consistently highlighted for its interesting color story that balanced neutral tones with unique, high-impact finishes.

2.  **Edward Scissorhands Palette by Melt Cosmetics** — 8 mentions  
    Fueled by excitement for the movie collaboration, this palette was frequently praised for its "grungy winter dream" color story of cool-toned blues, purples, and greens. Creators lauded the intricate snow-globe packaging and noted the shimmers were some of the best the brand has ever produced, describing them as exceptionally "shiny and glossy looking." The palette was the centerpiece of multiple tutorials and was ranked as a top favorite holiday release by one creator.

3.  **Ice Crystal Palette by Oden's Eye** — 5 mentions  
    Creators appreciated this palette for its "iridescent wintery vibes," featuring a unique mix of icy tones and warm pops of color. It was frequently mentioned for its beautiful shimmers that sparkle intensely in the light, making it a "pleasant surprise" for creators who enjoyed the creative looks it inspired. While some found the packaging bulky, the quality and unique color combinations garnered positive reviews.

4.  **Night of the Leonids Collection by Terra Moons Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    This collection of single shadows was heavily featured by swatch-focused creators on Instagram, who showcased its high-impact, sparkly, and multichrome formulas. The collection was praised for its cohesive range, offering everything from light, ethereal shimmers to dark, moody jewel tones. Creators highlighted the smooth texture and intense visual appeal of the shadows through detailed arm and eye swatches.

5.  **Reverie Collection by MOB Beauty** — 4 mentions  
    Launched in collaboration with creator Courtney Quarantine, this collection was described as having a cozy, warm, and nostalgic feel. The "Linger" lip gloss was a standout, noted for its unique grungy gray shade that was surprisingly wearable and flattering. The collection's cream clay shadow was also praised for its versatility, being used effectively as both an eyeshadow and a blush.

6.  **Gingerbread Bakery Palette by Simply Posh Cosmetics** — 4 mentions  
    This palette was described as having a "cutesy" theme, with a color story split between warm neutrals and sweet pastels. Creators consistently praised the high-quality formulas, noting the mattes were beautiful and blendable, while the shimmers were glossy and shiny. It was recommended as a great option for those who enjoy warm neutrals but want to "dabble in color."

7.  **FauxFilter Concealer by Huda Beauty** — 4 mentions  
    This concealer appeared as a staple product in multiple creator routines for spot concealing and brightening the under-eye area. One creator expressed strong positive sentiment, noting she loved the formula and was actively trying to finish her tube. Its consistent use in GRWMs and tutorials indicates it is a reliable, high-performance product for many.

8.  **Reap Lip Pencil by Unearthly Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This lip pencil was consistently featured as the foundation for dark, vampy, and grungy lip combos in short-form videos. Creators demonstrated its ability to provide a precise, pigmented matte base that paired effectively with contrasting glosses and metallic lipsticks. Its performance was praised for creating a "really cool contrast and effect" in multi-product lip looks.

9.  **Come Play With Us Palette by Bella Beaute Bar** — 3 mentions  
    Creators were enthusiastic about this palette's unique "spooky holiday" theme, which blended icy winter blues with classic reds and greens. The brand's formula was described as "fantastic," with creators highlighting the highly pigmented mattes and beautiful, sparkly shimmers. The palette's versatility was showcased in looks ranging from bold, monochromatic red eyes to more complex color stories.

10. **Merry Chrissy Palette by Glaminatrix Cosmetics** — 3 mentions  
    This new release generated buzz on Instagram for its unique "Australian Christmas" theme, with a color story and shade names inspired by Aussie culture. Initial swatches and reveals showcased pigmented mattes and a variety of sparkly, shimmery finishes. The discussion centered on the fun, nostalgic theme combined with the brand's reputation for high-quality formulas.
