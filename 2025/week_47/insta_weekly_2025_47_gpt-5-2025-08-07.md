## 5. Opportunities for the Store

- Stocking Recommendations:
  - Eyes:
    - Multichrome/iridescent singles and topper palettes: jewel olives, blackened greens, cranberry shimmers, icy silvers; include low‑flake, high‑sparkle options.
    - Mixed-metals duo sets (champagne + bronze/olive) and chartreuse pencils/liners.
    - Glitter support: high-grip eye-safe adhesives; non-lifting glitter gels.
  - Lips:
    - Cool brown and black liners, greige/gray glosses, blue-toned balms; deep aubergine sheers; metallic options.
    - High-sparkle gloss melts with varying particle sizes (offer texture transparency).
  - Complexion/Cheeks:
    - Baby pink and mauve/berry cream blushes (highly pigmented but blendable).
    - Duochrome/pearl highlighters (fire-opal, lavender/blue shifts).
    - Dewy SPF tone-up preps (semi vs wet finishes).
  - Tools/Extras:
    - Depotting/repair kits and magnetic palettes (Artificer buzz validates category).
    - Holiday-forward curated bundles: “Grunge Sparkle Kit” (black base, jewel topper, greige gloss, chartreuse liner, glitter adhesive).

- Content Strategy:
  - 30–60s try-on reels swatching “every shade” (Terra Moons-style) and low-light sparkle flips.
  - “Semi vs wet” dewy prep split-face tests; macro swatch labels; texture disclosures.
  - Lip cocktail recipes (liner + gloss + balm overlays) and under-brow/under-eye pastel placement shorts.
  - Mixed-metals eye tutorial; monochrome red look; “office-to-party” topper transitions.
  - Address shipping/tariff FAQs for AU brands; US-warehouse flags on PDPs.

- Gap Analysis:
  - Provide non-lifting glitter gels/adhesives and warn users about base-lifting formulas.
  - Offer KIKO 36 Cold Brown dupes (cool brown liners) and stock black liners with slip + set.
  - Include deeper anchor shades in pastel/cute palettes.
  - Note gloss texture options (buttery vs chunky glitter) and scent-free variants.
